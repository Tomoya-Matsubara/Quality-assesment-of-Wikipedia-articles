Vadim Demidov (born 10 October 1986 in Riga, Latvian SSR) is a Norwegian football player of Russophone descent who currently plays for Rosenborg BK. He formerly played for IL Runar, Sandefjord Fotball, Manglerud Star and H<U+00F8>nefoss BK. His father is former Soviet international handball player Sergei Demidov.
