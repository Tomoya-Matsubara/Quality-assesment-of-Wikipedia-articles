Jamie Hislop (born January 20, 1954 in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada) is a retired Canadian ice hockey forward.

Hislop started his National Hockey League career with the Quebec Nordiques in 1979. He also played for the Calgary Flames. He retired the NHL after the 1984 season. He later became an assistant coach for the Calgary Flames.
