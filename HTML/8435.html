Odette Siko was a French driver, who was active in the late 1920's. In 1932 she finished in 4th place in the 24 Hours of Le Mans, and won the 2L class. Siko started 3 times more in Le Mans. After 1933, she participated in some rallys with Hell<U+00E9> Nice.
