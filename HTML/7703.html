KZXL (96.3 FM, Cat Country) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country music format.1 Licensed to Hudson, Texas, USA, the station serves the Lufkin-Nacogdoches area. The station is currently owned by The Turning Leaf LLC.2

History

The station was assign the call letters KLSN on 1995-09-01. on 2001-10-12, the station changed its call sign to KLSN, on 2008-07-04 to KLJK, on 2009-03-03 to the current KZXL,3
