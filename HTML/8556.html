"On a Night Like This" is a song written by Bob Dylan. It first appeared on Dylan's 14th studio album, Planet Waves.1 It later appeared on Dylan, a greatest hits collection.

Los Lobos recorded a version of the song for the soundtrack of the 2003 film Masked and Anonymous.
