Didier Ibrahim Ndong is a Gabonese footballer who plays for CS Sfaxien in the Tunisian League. He plays as a Midfielder. In 2013 Anicet Yala, coach of the Gabon national U-20 team, called him up to be a member of the Democratic Republic of the Congo national under-20 football team for the 2013 African Youth Championship in Algeria.1
