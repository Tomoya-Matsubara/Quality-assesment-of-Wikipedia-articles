David Wall born 1 January, 1983 is best known as an Australian race car driver. As the son for former V8 Supercar driver and 2009 Australian Sports Sedan Champion Des Wall, he started in sedan cars rather the the conventional formula cars.

His mainstay has been sports car racing, particularly Porsches. He has won the Australian GT Championship twice in 2009 and 2010driving a Porsche 997 GT3 Cup S as well as competed in the Australian Carrera Cup Championship. He has also won the Australain Tourist Trophy in 2009 and 2010.

He has dabbled in Production Cars finishing third in Class A of the Bathurst 12 Hour race in 2008.

In 2009 he followed his father into V8 Supercars competing in the long distance races and in 2011 doing a full season in the Fujitsu V8 Supercar Series. 1 He is looking to join the series full time in 2012. 2
