Hadan (, also Romanized as H<U+0101>d<U+0101>n and Had<U+0101>n; also known as Ard<U+016B>n, H<U+0101>d<U+016B>n, and H<U+0101>v<U+0101>n)1 is a village in Sardshir Rural District, in the Central District of Buin va Miandasht County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 498, in 115 families.2
