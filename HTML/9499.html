Cosmin Mihai Pa<U+015F>covici (born April 12, 1978 in Suceava, Romania) is a Romanian football player who plays for Romanian Liga I side UTA Arad. Pa<U+015F>covici also played for other Liga I clubs like Farul Constan<U+0163>a and Dinamo Bucure<U+015F>ti. While playing in Constan<U+0163>a, he was considered one of the best defenders in the country.
