Jos<U+00E9> Antonio Guillermo Divito, also known simply as Divito (Buenos Aires, July 16, 1914 - Lajes, Brazil, July 5, 1969) was an illustrator, cartoonist, caricaturist and editor who, through his comic illustrations and humor had great influence in the decades from 1940 to 1960. He was the founder and director of the famous magazine Rico Tipo.1
