F<U+00E1>bio Alexandre Duarte Fel<U+00ED>cio (born on 2 May, 1982 in Faro) is a Portuguese football midfielder currently playing for CS Mar<U+00ED>timo in the Portuguese top-flight BWINLIGA. He was signed in the summer of 2007 from FC Rubin Kazan in Russia, and has also played for Spanish giants Real Sociedad.
