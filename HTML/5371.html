State Route 233 is a route from State Route 3 in the town square of Bar Harbor to State Routes 3 and 198 in Mount Desert. Route 233 is a bypass of the village of Northeast Harbor. The entire route is on Mount Desert Island. It has scenic views of Acadia National Park. The route is 5.8 miles long (9.3 km long). It would take about 10 minutes to travel the entire route with no traffic.
