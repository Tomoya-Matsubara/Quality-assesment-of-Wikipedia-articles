Othorene is a genus of moths of the Saturniidae family.

Systematics

The composition of the genus include:

  • Othorene cadmus (Herrich-Sch<U+00E4>ffer, 1854)
  • Othorene purpurascens (Schaus, 1905) <U+2014> Mexico
  • Othorene verana (Schaus, 1900) <U+2014> from Mexico to Panama and Guatemala
