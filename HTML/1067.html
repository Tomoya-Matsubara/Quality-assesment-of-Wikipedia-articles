Georgios Dedas (Greek: <U+0393><U+03B9><U+03CE><U+03C1><U+03B3><U+03BF><U+03C2> <U+0394><U+03AD><U+03B4><U+03B1><U+03C2>; born January 2, 1980 in Komotini, Greece) is a Greek professional basketball player. He is 2.00 m (6 ft 6 <U+00BE> in) tall and he weighs 100 kg (220 lbs). He plays at the small forward position.

Pro career

Dedas began his professional career in the 1999 with MENT. In 2003, he moved to Iraklis Thessaloniki. In 2006, he joined Breogan Lugo. He then moved to Beirasar Rosalia in 2007. He then joined Caceres 2016 CB in 2009. In 2010, he moved to PAOK Thessaloniki.

Greek national team

Dedas won the silver medal at the 1999 FIBA Europe Under-16 Championship and the gold medal at the 2002 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship as a member of the junior Greek national basketball teams. He also won the silver medal at the 2005 Mediterranean Games, while playing with the senior Greek national basketball team.

Awards and accomplishments

  • 1999 FIBA Europe Under-16 Championship - Silver Medal
  • 2002 FIBA Europe Under-20 Championship - Gold Medal
  • 2005 Mediterranean Games - Silver Medal
