Kavanak (, also Romanized as Kav<U+0101>nak)1 is a village in Mizdej-e Olya Rural District, in the Central District of Farsan County, Chaharmahal and Bakhtiari Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 339, in 63 families.2
