Bystroistoksky District () is an administrative and municipal district (raion), one of the sixty in Altai Krai, Russia. Its administrative center is the rural locality (a selo) of Bystry Istok.1 District's population: Population of Bystry Istok accounts for 36.0% of the district's population.2
