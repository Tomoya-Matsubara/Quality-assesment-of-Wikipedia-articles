The Coupe de France Final 1952 was a football match held at Stade Olympique Yves-du-Manoir, Colombes on May 4, 1952, that saw OGC Nice defeat FC Girondins de Bordeaux 5-3 thanks to goals by Victor Nuremberg ,Luis Carniglia, Jean Belver, Abdelaziz Ben Tifour and Georges C<U+00E9>sari.
