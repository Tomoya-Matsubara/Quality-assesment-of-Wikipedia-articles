Monroeville Vineyard & Winery is a winery in the Monroeville section of Upper Pittsgrove Township in Salem County, New Jersey.12 The vineyard was first planted in 2010, and opened to the public in 2012.34 Monroeville has 4 acres of grapes under cultivation, and produces wine from New Jersey and Chilean grapes.56

Wines

Monroeville Vineyard is in the Outer Coastal Plain AVA, and produces wine from Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon, Chardonnay, Concord, Gr<U+00FC>ner Veltliner, Merlot, Muscat, Muscat of Alexandria, Pinot gris, and Syrah grapes.78 Monroeville also makes fruit wines from apples, blueberries, cranberries, nectarines, peaches, strawberries.9

Licensing and associations

Monroeville has a plenary winery license from the New Jersey Division of Alcoholic Beverage Control, which allows it to produce an unrestricted amount of wine, operate up to 15 off-premises sales rooms, and ship up to 12 cases per year to consumers in-state or out-of-state.1011 The winery is a member of the Garden State Wine Growers Association, and the Outer Coastal Plain Vineyard Association.1213
