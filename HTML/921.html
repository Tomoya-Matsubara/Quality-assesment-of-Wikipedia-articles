Chris Llewellyn is an American poet.

Life

She graduated from Warren Wilson College.

Her work appeared in Pudding House.1

She married Justice Department lawyer Edward Bordley. They live in a small Northeast Washington, D.C. apartment,2 and have a daughter, Elizabeth Bordley.3

  • 1986 Walt Whitman Award
