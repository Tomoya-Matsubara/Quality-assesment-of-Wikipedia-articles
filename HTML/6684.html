AL HASSAN al-mustadi bin YUSUF al-mustanjid (1142 - 1180) (Arabic: <U+0627><U+0644><U+0645><U+0633><U+062A><U+0636><U+0626> <U+0628><U+0623><U+0645><U+0631> <U+0627><U+0644><U+0644><U+0647>) was the Abbasid Caliph in Baghdad from 1170 to 1180. Like his predecessor, he continued to occupy a more or less independent position, with a Vazir and courtly surroundings, and supported by only a small force sufficient for an occasional local campaign.during his reign saladin,ended the fatemid caliphate,and became the sultan of Egypt and declared his allegiance to the abbasids.Ibn jawzy-famous chronicle- said about him :he reduced the taxes,and was very fair with his sujects,built many mosques,schools,ribats''. and in 1080 caliph Al-mustadi dies and was succeeded by his son Ahmed (an-nasir)
