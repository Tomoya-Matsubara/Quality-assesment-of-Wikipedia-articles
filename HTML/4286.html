Ney Island is an uninhabited island in Qikiqtaaluk Region, Nunavut, Canada. Located in Hudson Bay, it is a member of the Belcher Islands group. Other islands in the immediate vicinity include:1

  • Karlay Island
  • Mata Island
  • Nero Island
  • Dove Island
  • Bradbury Island
