Nemai Ghosh, (b. 1934) is noted Indian photographer most known for working with Satyajit Ray.1 as a still photographer for over two decades, starting with Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne (1969) till Ray's last filmAgantuk (1991).23

He was a jury member at the 2007 National Film Awards,45 and was awarded the Padma Shri by Government of India in 2010.6
