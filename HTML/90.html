Diego Ruiz (born 5 February 1982) is a Spanish middle distance runner who specializes in the 1500 metres.

He won the silver medal at the 2009 European Indoor Championships.1

Personal bests

  • 400 metres - 47.67 s (2001)
  • 800 metres - 1:46.40 min (2008)
  • 1500 metres - 3:35.40 min (2008)
  • Mile run - 4:00.35 min (2008)
  • 3000 metres - 7:58.08 min (2009, indoor)
