Taylor Thomas Lindsey (born December 2, 1991) is a professional baseball player in the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim organization. He is the 93rd prospect by Baseball America.1

Lindsey was drafted 37th overall by the Los Angeles Angels of Anaheim in the 2010 Major League Baseball Draft, out of Desert Mountain High School.2
