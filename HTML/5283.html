In the fictional Archie Universe, Archie's Gang refers to the collection of people consisting of Archie Andrews, Betty Cooper, Jughead Jones, Veronica Lodge, Dilton Doiley and Reggie Mantle.

Honorary members of the gang include Hot Dog

The gang frequently hangs out at Pop Tate's establishment, performs before live audiences as The Archies, perform various school club duties at Riverdale High School, and spend vacation time together.
