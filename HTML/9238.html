Starkey School is a historic school building located at Cave Spring, Roanoke County, Virginia. It was built about 1915, and is thought to incorporate an earlier one-room school built about 1894. It is a brick school building flanked by wings built in 1928, and measuring 24 feet by 33 feet. In 1961, the school was closed and was purchased by Roanoke County.1

It was added to the National Register of Historic Places in 2002.2
