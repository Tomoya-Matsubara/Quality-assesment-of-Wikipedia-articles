Deh-e Now-ye Ali Shahri (, also Romanized as Deh-e Now-ye <U+2018>Al<U+012B> Shahr<U+012B>)1 is a village in Horjand Rural District, Kuhsaran District, Ravar County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 62, in 17 families.2
