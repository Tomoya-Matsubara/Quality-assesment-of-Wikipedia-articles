At the Kreuzjoch is is the highest peak in the Kitzb<U+00FC>hel Alps in the Austrian state of Tyrol.

The Kreuzjoch also forms the southwestern end of the Kitzb<U+00FC>hel Alps. To the west it is bounded by the Ziller valley, to the southeast by the Gerlos valley. The Kreuzjoch's neighbouring peak is the Torhelm ().
