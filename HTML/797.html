Horn<U+00FD> Lieskov is a village and municipality in Pova<U+017E>sk<U+00E1> Bystrica District in the Tren<U+010D><U+00ED>n Region of north-western Slovakia.

History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1330.

Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 370 metres and covers an area of 4.897 km<U+00B2>. It has a population of about 373 people.
