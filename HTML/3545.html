Mime Glacier () is a small glacier at the south (upper) end of Tiw Valley in the Asgard Range, Victoria Land. The name is one in a group given by New Zealand Antarctic Place-Names Committee (NZ-APC) from Norse mythology. In Der Ring des Nibelungen, Mime is the smith who aids Siegfried to win the ring and is slain by the hero for his treachery.
