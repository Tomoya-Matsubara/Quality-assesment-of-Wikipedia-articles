W<U+0119>gierskie is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pot<U+0119>gowo, within S<U+0142>upsk County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.1 It lies approximately north-east of Pot<U+0119>gowo, east of S<U+0142>upsk, and west of the regional capital Gda<U+0144>sk.

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.

The village has a population of 85.
