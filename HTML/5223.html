Hajji Lak (, also Romanized as <U+1E28><U+0101>jj<U+012B> Lak)1 is a village in Akhtachi-ye Mahali Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 749, in 153 families.2
