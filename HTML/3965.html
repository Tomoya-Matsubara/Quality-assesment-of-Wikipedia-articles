Carlos Campos (sometimes listed as Guto, born July 5, 1978) is a Brazilian sprint canoer who competed in the early 2000s. At the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney, he was eliminated in the semifinals of both the K-2 500 m and the K-2 1000 m events.
