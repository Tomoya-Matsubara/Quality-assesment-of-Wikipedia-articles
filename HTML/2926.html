St<U+00E9>phane Breton (L<U+00E9>vis, Qu<U+00E9>bec) is a Canadian actor.

He graduated from the Conservatoire d'art dramatique de Montr<U+00E9>al in 1996.

Filmography

He has appeared in various TV series including:

  • Smash (TV series) (as Martial the trainer of Fran<U+00E7>ois)
  • 2 fr<U+00E8>res (Michel Guilbert)
  • Mon meilleur ennemi (Michel Lebeau)

In film he has appeared in:

  • Le Petit ciel (2000),
  • Qu<U+00E9>bec-Montr<U+00E9>al (2002)1
  • Nez Rouge (2003).
  • Il <U+00E9>tait une fois dans le trouble in the role of R<U+00E9>al
  • Le Bonheur des autres (film) in 2011 as Yves
  • Camion 2012 directed by Rafa<U+00EB>l Ouellet2
