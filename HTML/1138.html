Pieski is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Mi<U+0119>dzyrzecz, within Mi<U+0119>dzyrzecz County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland.1 It lies approximately west of Mi<U+0119>dzyrzecz and north of Zielona G<U+00F3>ra.

The village has a population of 245 (population in 2008).
