Patrick Konchellah (born April 20, 1968) is a former Kenyan middle distance runner who finished fourth in the 800 metres final at the 1997 World Championships in Athletics in Athens. The race was won by Wilson Kipketer.

Konchellah's personal best time is 1:42.98 minutes.
