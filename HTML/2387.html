Cory-Rawson High School is a public high school in Rawson, Ohio. It is the only high school in the Cory-Rawson Schools district. It is named for the villages of Mount Cory and Rawson. Their nickname is the Hornets. School colors are yellow and green. They are a member of the Blanchard Valley Conference.
