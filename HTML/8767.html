Michel Vorm (born October 20, 1983 in Nieuwegein) is a Dutch footballer, currently playing for F.C. Utrecht. his position is goalkeeper.

He spent some time on loan at F.C. Den Bosch.

In 2006 he was part of the Netherlands squad that won the UEFA U-21 Championship 2006 in Portugal.
