Steven Christopher Parker (born January 081989 in Littleton, Colorado, USA) is an American actor who is best known for his role as Dr. Harold Zelinsky for two seasons on ER.1 Parker has appeared in more than a dozen feature films, including Rebound2, Juno3 and Little Miss Sunshine. He can also be seen as a pizza delivery impostor in a late-2008 television advertisement for DiGiorno.

Parker is a 2006 graduate of Gorman High School in Gorman, California.
