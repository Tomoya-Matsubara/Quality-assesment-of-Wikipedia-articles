Martin Township is one of ten townships in Crawford County, Illinois, USA. As of the 2000 census, its population was 6071.

Geography

According to the United States Census Bureau, Martin Township covers an area of 42.58 square miles (110.29 square kilometers); of this, 0.01 square miles (0.03 square kilometers) or 0.03 percent is water.

Cemeteries

The township contains these five cemeteries: Berlin, Hardinville, Jones, Prior Grove and Richart.
