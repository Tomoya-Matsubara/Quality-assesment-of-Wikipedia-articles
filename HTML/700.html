Moghanlu (, also Romanized as Mogh<U+0101>nl<U+016B>)1 is a village in Qezel Gechilu Rural District, in the Central District of Mahneshan County, Zanjan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 522, in 124 families.2
