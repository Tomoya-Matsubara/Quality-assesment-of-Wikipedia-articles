Ericiolacerta was a small (8 inches/20 centimeters) therocephalian therapsid from the early Triassic of South Africa. The fossils suggest that it was a small, arboreal/terrestrial carnivore, possibly hunting in packs.

Popular Culture

Ericiolacerta was seen in the 3rd episode of Walking With Monsters. It was shown having venom, but there is no fossil evidence to prove that.. The CGI model of this animals is actually very similar to that of the cynodonts of the documentary Walking With Dinosaurs.
