Qeshlaq-e Khan Goldi Davakishi (, also Romanized as Qeshl<U+0101>q-e Kh<U+0101>n Gold<U+012B> Da<U+02BF>v<U+0101>k<U+012B>sh<U+012B>) is a village in Qeshlaq-e Sharqi Rural District, Qeshlaq Dasht District, Bileh Savar County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 49, in 11 families.1
