Niketa Barker (born 1991) is an Guyanese beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Guyana 2014 and will represent her country at Miss Universe 2014 in USA.

Early life

Barker is a model.

Pageantry

Miss Guyana 2014

The 23-year-old beauty, Niketa Barker was crowned Miss Guyana Universe,1 Sunday evening at the poolside of the Pegasus Hotel in Kingston, Georgetown.

Miss Universe 2014

Barker, will represent Guyana at the Miss Universe 2014 pageant to be held on January 25, in Doral, USA.
