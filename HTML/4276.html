Break the Pot is the upcoming second studio album by American rapper Rich Boy. The album is set to be released on April 9, 2013.1 The album features guest appearances from Mista Raja, Maino, Hemi, GQ Beats, Doe B, Playboi Lo, Smash, Maja, Kaleena, Jue and Bobby V.2

Background

On January 14, 2013, the first single was released in promotion of the album titled "Break the Pot" featuring Hemi.3 On February 25, 2013, it was announced the album would be released on April 9, 2013, and would feature guest appearances from Maino, Mista Raja, Bobby V, Doe B, Playboi Lo and Smash.4 On March 17, 2013, the music video for "Break the Pot" featuring Hemi premiered on MTV Jams.5 On March 18, 2012, Rich Boy released the mixtape Back To Class in promotion for the album.6
