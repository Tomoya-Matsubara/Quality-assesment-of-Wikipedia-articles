Carlos P<U+00E9>rez Su<U+00E1>rez (born July 14, 1994 in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico) is a Mexican professional boxer in the Light Welterweight division.1 Carlos is promoted by current WBC Champion, Mexican Sa<U+00FA>l <U+00C1>lvarez' company Canelo Promotions.2

Amateur career

During his amateur career he had seventeen fights, going 16-1.3

Professional career

In December 2010, P<U+00E9>rez beat the veteran Ivan Rodriguez at the Arena Jalisco in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico.4

In his next fight Carlos lost against the veteran Cesar Figueroa, on the undercard Sa<U+00FA>l <U+00C1>lvarez vs. Ryan Rhodes.5
