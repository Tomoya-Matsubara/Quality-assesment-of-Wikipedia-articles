Urumqi Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Fair (), commonly abbreviated as Urumqi Fair (), is an annual trade fair held in Urumqi of Xinjiang, China, since it was started on September 2, 1992.1 Its purpose is to promote trading and market exploration in Northwestern China with foreign countries mainly in Central Asia. Urumqi Fair is the largest trade fair in Western China.

The 17th Urumqi Foreign Economic Relations and Trade Fair, the latest event, was held in September 2008. It is the first time that the fair seeing participants from the Ministry of Commerce and the China Council for Promotion of International Trade.2
