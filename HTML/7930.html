Broken Wing is an EP by American punk rock band Alkaline Trio, released on April 2, 2013 on Epitaph and Heart & Skull. Produced by both Bill Stevenson and Jason Livermore, Broken Wing was recorded during the same sessions that yielded the band's ninth studio album, My Shame Is True, and was released on the same day. The EP predominantly features songs sung by bassist and vocalist Dan Andriano.

Digital deluxe versions of My Shame Is True include the four songs from Broken Wing as bonus tracks.1
