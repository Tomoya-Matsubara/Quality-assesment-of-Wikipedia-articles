Salgaocar SC is an Indian football team based in the state of Goa. They are currently playing in the 10th ONGC National Football League. They play their home matches at the Jawaharlal Nehru-Fatorda stadium (capacity 35,000) in Margao, Goa. Founded in 1955, it is sponsored by the V.M. Salgaocar Group of Companies. The club is associated with the National Football League, and is currently in the Premier Division of that league.
