Jackie Filgo and Jeff Filgo are a married screenwriting and television writing team.1

In television, their credits include Ink, the American adaptation of Men Behaving Badly, That '70s Show, Happy Hour (also creators), The New Adventures of Old Christine and Hank.

In film, they co-wrote screenplay for Diary of a Wimpy Kid (2010) and wrote the screenplay for Take Me Home Tonight (2011), starring Topher Grace, who they previously worked with on That '70s Show].2
