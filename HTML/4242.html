Su Dongshui, chief professor of Fudan University, Shanghai, China, and the chair of IFSAM 9th world conference(2008), shanghai, china, is the founder of the oriental management school. Oriental Management is a new research branch in the world academia of management science. As an experienced scholar, Prof. Su Dongshui has been engaged in the teaching and scientific studies in Shanghai Academy of Social Sciences, Shanghai University of Finance Economics and Fudan University successively, after his graduation from Xiamen University in 1953. During his more than 50 years of teaching career, he has brought up more than 200 masters, over 80 doctors and 40 post-doctors. Many of his students have become leaders of governments, colleges or universities and enterprises.
