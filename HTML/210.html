The 2012 Fukuoka International Women's Cup was a professional tennis tournament played on grass courts. It was the twelfth edition of the tournament which was part of the 2012 ITF Women's Circuit. It took place in Fukuoka, Japan, on 7<U+2013>13 May 2012.
