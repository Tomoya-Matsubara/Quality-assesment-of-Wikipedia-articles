The 2013<U+2013>14 Cincinnati Saints season is the first season in the Professional Arena Soccer League (PASL) for the Cincinnati Saints professional indoor soccer club. The Saints, an Eastern Division team, play their home games at the Tri-County Soccerplex in Cincinnati, Ohio. The team is led by general manager Mackenzie Long and head coach Matt Brienes with assistant coach Kyle Kammer.

Season summary

The Cincinnati Saints participated in the 2013<U+2013>14 United States Open Cup for Arena Soccer starting with a bye in the Round of 32 and a 10<U+2013>6 loss to the Cleveland Freeze in the Round of 16, ending their tournament run.

History

The Saints had previously been a member of the affiliated amateur Premier Arena Soccer League before moving up the pro league in 2013.12 Starting in 2014, the Saints organization will play outdoor soccer during the PASL offseason as an expansion team in the Great Lakes Conference of the National Premier Soccer League.3
