Estrada de Ferro Central do Brasil is a main railway station located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The station is responsible for linking Rio de Janeiro with the main cities of S<U+00E3>o Paulo and Belo Horizonte.

The station is not to be confused with the Estrada de Ferro Central do Brasil, formerly one of the principal railways of Brazil.
