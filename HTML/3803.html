1 West India Quay is a skyscraper in the Docklands area of London which was completed in 2004. It is 111 metres (364 feet) tall and has 34 floors. The bottom 12 floors house a Marriott Hotel, including 47 serviced suites on floors 9-12. Floors 13-33 house 158 apartments. The tower overlooks West India Docks and Canary Wharf.
