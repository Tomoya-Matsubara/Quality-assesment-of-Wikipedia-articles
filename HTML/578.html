Conus lignarius is a species of sophisticated predatory sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk, more popularly known as a cone snail, cone shell or cone.

Also see Cone snail.

Distribution

This is an Indo-Pacific species.

The type locality is Port Sacloban, Leyte Island in the Philippines.
