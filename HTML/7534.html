Biaraq (, also Romanized as B<U+012B><U+2018>araq; also known as B<U+012B><U+2018>areh)1 is a village in Ani Rural District, in the Central District of Germi County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 44, in 8 families.2
