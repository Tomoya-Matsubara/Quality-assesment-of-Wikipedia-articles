Kait<U+014D> Apricot (<U+602A><U+76D7><U+30A2><U+30D7><U+30EA><U+30B3><U+30C3><U+30C8> Kait<U+014D> Apurikotto, translates to Mysterious Thief Apricot) is a GxB visual novel (or otome game) video game for Microsoft Windows. The game was created by Takuyo. It was later re-released for the PS2 and the PSP with an extra character, Kyosuke Kiryu.
