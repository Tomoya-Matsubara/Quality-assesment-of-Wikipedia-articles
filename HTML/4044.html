John A. Farrell Stadium is a stadium in West Chester, Pennsylvania. It is primarily used by the West Chester University of Pennsylvania Golden Rams football and track & field teams.1 The stadium was also the temporary home of the Philadelphia Independence of the Women's Professional Soccer league while their new stadium, PPL Park, was finishing construction.2
