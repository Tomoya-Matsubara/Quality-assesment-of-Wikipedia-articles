Tor Egil Moxness Horn (born 1 April 1976) is a former Norwegian football goalkeeper who played his entire professional career for Norwegian team F.K. Bod<U+00F8>/Glimt.

He joined the team from Br<U+00F8>nn<U+00F8>ysund Idrettslag in 1996, made his Norwegian Premier League debut in 1997 and became the first-team regular in 2001. He retired in March 2007 due to a long time knee injury.
