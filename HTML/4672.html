WCKT is a commercial radio station located in Lehigh Acres, Florida, broadcasting to the Fort Myers, Florida area on 107.1 FM. WCKT airs a country music format branded as "Cat Country". On March 21, 2007 WCKT moved from 100.1 FM to 107.1 FM, swapping frequencies with smooth jazz-formatted WZJZ.
