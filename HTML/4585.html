The Golden Disk Awards is a awards show founded in 1986 that is presented annually by the Music Industry Association of Korea for outstanding achievements in the music industry in South Korea.1

Trophy

The trophy is called "Statue of a Woman Playing a Reed Instrument" () that was designed by professor Kim Soo-Hyun ().2

Categories

Currently there are seven awards which are not restricted by genre, and two awards which are each in hip hop, trot.

  • Grand Prize is awarded to the performer of the best of the best album belong to "Records of the Year".
  • Grand Prize of Digital Releasing is awarded to the performer of the best of the best album which was digital released, belong to "Records of the Year".
  • Records of the Year are awarded to the performers of top 10 full albums of the year.
  • Record Producer of the Year is awarded to a record producer of the year.
  • Hip Hop Record of the Year is awarded to the performer of the hip hop album of the year.
  • Trot Record of the Year is awarded to the performer of the trot album of the year.
  • Popularity Awards of the Year are awarded to the performers who gained popularity of the year.
  • Newbie Awards of the Year are awarded to the newbie performers of the year.
  • Special Award is awarded to the performer or the production team who gained popularity or outstanding achievement in miscellaneous topics of Korean music industry.
