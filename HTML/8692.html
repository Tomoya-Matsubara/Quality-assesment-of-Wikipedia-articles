Aaron John Williams (born 19 October 1993, West Bromwich, England) is an English professional footballer currently playing for Walsall as a striker.

Career

Williams was born in West Bromwich, in the metropolitan borough of Sandwell, West Midlands. He grew up in nearby Wednesbury and attended Wodensborough Community Technology College. He trained with Manchester United as a youngster but later joined Walsall, a club closer to his home. He joined up with Redditch United on a work experience loan deal in early 2012, before signing a one-year professional contract with Walsall ahead of the 2012<U+2013>13 season.1

He was later handed the #19 shirt and made his professional debut as a second-half substitute for Nicky Featherstone on 21 August 2012 against Oldham Athletic.2
