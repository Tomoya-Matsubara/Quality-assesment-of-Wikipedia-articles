The Landana Firefinch (Lagonosticta landanae), also known as the Pale-billed Firefinch, is a species of estrildid finch found in central Africa. It has an estimated global extent of occurrence of 100,000 km<U+00B2>. It is found in Angola, the Republic of Congo, the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The status of the species is evaluated as Least Concern.
