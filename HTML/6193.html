Qaleh Sangi (, also Romanized as Qal<U+2018>eh Sang<U+012B>)1 is a village in Robat Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 979, in 199 families.2
