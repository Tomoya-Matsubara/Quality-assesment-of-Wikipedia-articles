Adriano Laaber Manfred, better known as Adrianinho (Jundiai, July 11, 1980), is a Brazilian footballer.

Career

Adriano Manfred Laaber is half-handed style creator. Revealed by Ponte Preta and Corinthians and Flamengo crossings, was hired by Brasiliense.

In 2003, when he was still on the bridge, came to be probed to defend Austria, land of his father. However, although he accepted the invitation of the Austria squad (which would be the first Brazilian), did not play for her.

It was revealed by Ponte Preta when he was only 18 years old, and eventually gained the status of promise, which made him pass for Corinthians and also the team's Most Twisted Brazil, Flamengo.
