Lars Waage Hansen (born 24 February 1992) is a Norwegian footballer who plays for <U+00C5>sane.

He started his career in SK Vard Haugesund, and played the 2011 season for Norwegian Premier League club FK Haugesund.1 After the 2011 season he moved to Bergen to study.2 He chose to play for <U+00C5>sane Fotball.
