The RT-20 was an intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM) developed but not deployed by the Soviet Union during the Cold War. It was assigned the NATO reporting name SS-15 Scrooge and carried the industry designation 8K99.

The RT-20 was the first mobile ICBM designed by the Soviets.

Operator

  • : The Strategic Rocket Forces was the only operator of the RT-20.
