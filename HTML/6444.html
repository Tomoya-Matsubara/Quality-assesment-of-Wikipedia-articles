Pijanowice is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Krobia, within Gosty<U+0144> County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.1 It lies approximately north of Krobia, south-west of Gosty<U+0144>, and south of the regional capital Pozna<U+0144>.

The village has a population of 168.
