Khar Farih-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Khar Far<U+012B><U+1E29>-e Sofl<U+00E1>; also known as <U+1E28>ar Far<U+012B><U+1E29>-e Sofl<U+00E1>)1 is a village in Abdoliyeh-ye Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Ramshir County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.2
