Gullsp<U+00E5>ng is a locality and one of the two seats of Gullsp<U+00E5>ng Municipality, V<U+00E4>stra G<U+00F6>taland County, Sweden, the other is Hova. It has 1,175 inhabitants in 2005.1

History

Gullsp<U+00E5>ng provided the name for the new municipality created through the local government reform of 1971, but the administration was placed in the more centrally located Hova, with just some minor offices in Gullsp<U+00E5>ng.

Due to the long lasting rivalry between Gullsp<U+00E5>ng and Hova the municipality avoids the term centralort (seat). Instead they are called the municipality's two main localities.

Business

In Gullsp<U+00E5>ngs municipality one finds the majority of industries and professionals. A major employer in the place is Partex Marking Systems, the company that manufactures cable identification is one of the largest exporters in Sweden of just cable labeling.

In Gullsp<U+00E5>ng Fortum also has a hydroelectric power as using the water from Skagern produces electricity. Although Fortum is quite a large employer in the town Gullsp<U+00E5>ng

A stone's throw from Gullsp<U+00E5>ng is V<U+00E4>nerplys factory for the manufacture of Plywood (Otterb<U+00E4>cken). It produces around 8 000 000m <U+00B2> Plywood year. The company has about 160 employees and is the largest employer in the municipality.

V<U+00E4>nerhamn AB, which has cargo ports around the entire V<U+00E4>nern is also a port of Otterb<U+00E4>cken. The port is the only natural deep harbor in the entire V<U+00E4>nern treat several million tonnes of freight each year.

Culture

Linda Bengtzing, who's a famous Swedish singer had in 2005 a hit with the song called "alla flickor" in the Swedish Song Contest. Linda was borned and raised in Gullsp<U+00E5>ng. She has participated in the Swedish Song Contest twice after "alla flickor".

From Hova, the famous songwriter, founder of the Polar Prize, and Abba's "fifth member" Stikkan Andersson. Stikkan died in 1997, but there is a statue in memory of Stikkan on the Square in Hova.

From 1939-1954 it was the famous "R<U+00F6>de Orm" author Frans G Bengtsson living on Ribbingsfors Mansion, just outside Gullsp<U+00E5>ng. There he wrote many of his famous work, and left behind many books, writings and notes which are now in a special part of the municipal library. The department called memory libraries and can also be regarded as a museum.

In recent years, the cultural work of the municipality taken very seriously and developed greatly. More information is available at Gullsp<U+00E5>ngs Tourist Office.

The salmon

Quite centrally located in Gullsp<U+00E5>ng can find Salmon-stair build in 2004. It was built to increase in population of the Salmon. Here you can in nice autumn days see the salmon spawn and jump. The stair built by the bottom of the river gradually Gullsp<U+00E5>ng has ran out and the small "pools" has been cast. This makes it easier for salmon to find the playground which is situated below floodgate. The Salmon-stair is built in a part of the river, which for years has been dry, although thanks to the dust gaps in the power flow can be regulated.
