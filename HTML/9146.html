Esther Smith is an English actress best known for her work in the television series Uncle and Cuckoo.

Early life

Smith was raised in Stourbridge, West Midlands. Her parents are teachers, and she has a sister, Rachel.12 Smith began dancing when she was three and was active in her local pantomime company.34 She studied ballet and contemporary dance at King Edward VI College in Stourbridge, where she began acting, before moving to the Guildford School of Acting at the urging of a teacher.56

Career

Shortly after leaving drama school, Smith rose to prominence in 2010 for her role as Trish in Material Girl.7 She has since had guest appearances on Skins and The Midnight Beast.

In 2014, she replaced Tamla Kari as Rachel for the second series of Cuckoo on BBC Three.8 She also starred in the ITV2 comedy Cockroaches and has a recurring role on another BBC Three sitcom, Uncle, as Melodie.910
