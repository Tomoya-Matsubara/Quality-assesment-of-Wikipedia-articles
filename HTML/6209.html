Factomania is a factual television series that is set to first broadcast on BBC Knowledge and BBC HD in 2014. The hosts for the ten-part series are Greg Foot, Fran Scott and Dominic Byrne.1234

Production

Factomania is a 360 Production for BBC Worldwide.5 BBC America included the series in its first co-production deal with BBC Worldwide.6 Lucy Pilkington is the executive producer for BBC Knowledge.78
