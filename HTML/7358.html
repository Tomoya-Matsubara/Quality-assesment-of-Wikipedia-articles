John SterneDD was appointed Bishop of Bedford under the provisions of the Suffragan Bishops Act 1534 in 1537 and held the post until his death in 15601. Educated at Cambridge University 2, from 1527 he was the Provincial of the English Dominicans and Prior of Sudbury. In the course of a turbulent Episcopate he assisted in the Consecration of several Bishops3.
