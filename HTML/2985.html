Annick Thoumazeau (born in Fr<U+00E9>jus in 1960) is a French singer. She represented France in the Eurovision Song Contest 1984, performing third on the night, before Spain, and after Luxembourg, with the song "Autant d'amoureux que d'<U+00E9>toiles", and placed eighth of nineteen, with sixty-one points1.
