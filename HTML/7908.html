Spider-Man Unlimited is an endless runner video game developed and published by Gameloft based on the Marvel Comics superhero Spider-Man. In the game, the player controls the title character and his alternate versions on his fight against the members of the Sinister Six and their multiverse counterparts. Although an endless runner, the main mode features definitive goals in constrat to the traditional aim of running as far as possible to get the highest score as possible. The game is also known for regularly having special, time-limited community events.

First announced in June 2014, Spider-Man Unlimited was released for Apple iOS, Android OS and Windows Phone on September 10, 2014. Starting from October 2014, Gameloft released four free updates that added new chapters to the story mode, Spider-Men and Spider-Women, enemies, events and stages. Since its release, it has been downloaded 30 million times and has been well-received by gaming critics. Reviewers praised controls, audio, animation, and the variety of characters, while criticized its energy system that they considered a limit to the playability.

Plot

After Spider-Man defeated a figure known as the Gold Goblin, Nick Fury tells him that the Green Goblin assembled a multiverse Sinister Six by a portal and plans to take over Spider-Man's dimension. S.H.I.E.L.D then used the portal to bring alternative versions of Spider-Man to aide the battle. Spider-Man goes after the Goblin and his alternative versions (Gold Goblin, Grey Goblin, Menace, House of M Goblin). After he defeated his arch-enemy's alternative versions, he fights various Vultures (Classic Vulture, Ultimate Vulture, Red Vulture, Dark Vulture). Electro and his alternatives versions (Classic Electro, Ultimate Electro, Modern Electro, Pure Energy Electro), Sandman and his alternate versions (Classic Sandman, Noir Sandman, Dark Sandman, and Pure Sand Sandman), and Doctor Octopus as well as the Sinister Soldiers (armored soldiers working for the Multiverse Sinister Six).

Gameplay

Spider-Man Unlimited is an endless runner game in which Spider-Man runs across New York rooftops<U+2014>and other scenarios such as the Oscorp building,1 Sinister Six spaceships or a giant Doctor Octopus machine2<U+2014>as he dodges obstacles, defeats enemies, and collect power-ups and vials.3 Regular enemies are defeated by slide attacks and punchs,45 while bosses requires the player to fling projectiles at them.6 Regardless the type of the enemey, it earns the player combos;7 by nearly hitting an obstacle and going through a ring one's combo count is also increased.8910 Interspersed with the running portions of the game are sections where Spider-Man swings through the streets, climbs up the sides of buildings, and free-falls off from the buildings.11

The game has a story mode that, in contrast to a traditional endless runner, features definitive goals1213<U+2014>for example, defeating a predetermined number of enemies, collecting random items, reaching a specific point, or defeating the boss.1415 It is divided into chapters, or "issues";16 each issue has five main missions which ends up with a boss fight,17 and various side missions.18 Certain missions are restricted to characters of a certain level,19 and others can only be completed by particular Spiders.20 Completion of missions enable the player to win experience points to level up and in-game currency<U+2014>regular vials or more rare ISO-8.21 By expending vials one can improve one's power-ups,22 and buy alternate versions of Spider-Man.23

To get new a Spider-Man<U+2014>which is a card24<U+2014>one can use one's vials or ISO-8 to open portals to an alternate dimension and a randomly selected Spider-Man arrives.2526 Each card can be leveled up and has a star rating27<U+2014>the level cap; the player can sacrifice a card to level up other and, by fusing two equal cards, the player can increase a card's level cap.2829 By using vials one has a small chance of acquiring a rare Spidey<U+2014>it gives the player a 3-4 star character30<U+2014>, while ISO-8 ensures a rare one.31 Each card has singular abilities<U+2014>for example, Spider-Armor gains 30% more score from running32<U+2014>and a score multiplier that is increased once a level is upped.3334 Each card occupies a character slot, which are limited to six at start.35 The player can unlock slots by completing an issue,36 or can buy it by expending vials and<U+2014>as the price increases<U+2014>ISO-8s.37

In addition to the story mode, the game features a de facto endless runner-style Unlimited Mode3839 and time-limited events;40 both are score-based games.41 Based on rank, the Unlimited leaderboard awards daily prizes.42 In the events, the player can compete against other players' scores and win rewards;43 ranking on certain positions of the leaderboard or reaching certain objectives earns the player a the Spider-Man featured in that event.44 Additionally, one can do extra missions<U+2014>called "Spidey Ops"45<U+2014>in which a character or more characters<U+2014>maximum of six46<U+2014>become unavailable for a set amount of time and when they return they gain experience and vials.47

The game has an energy system that gives the player five energy points at the start of the playing.48 It costs a point to go into a run in any mode, and it takes ten minutes to a point charge again.49 The player can also pay to refill energy by expending ISO-8s.5051 Alternatively, at the leaderboards, one can make friends, and, once per day, the player can send and request five energy points, which become stored at one's inbox.5253

Development and release

Spider-Man Unlimited' was developed and published by Paris-based Gameloft;54 it was directed by Baptiste Marmey, produced by Steve Melanson, designed by Corentin Delprat, programmed by Jerome Chen, and its music was composed by Pascal Dion.55 Through a press release on June 6, 2014, Gameloft announced a partnership with Marvel Entertainment and the development of a Spider-Man-based game for smartphones and tablets.56 Later that month, during the Electronic Entertainment Expo, an announcement trailer was exhibited,5758 and the game was made playable.59 A second trailer was shown at the San Diego Comic-Con International in July.60 It was released on September 10, 2014, for iOS, Android OS, and Windows Phone.6162

Updates

Gameloft released the first update to Spider-Man Unlimited in October 2014 and it added a fourth issue to the story mode with Sandman as the boss, a limited Halloween holiday event starring Jack O'Lantern, and Spider-Gwen and Spider-Girl as playable characters.63 It also included a New York Highline environment64 and bug fixes, and it was adapted to run on Windows Phone devices with 512 MB of RAM.65

A second update was released in December 2014; its main addition was the "Great Hunt" event, which features events to battle against the Inheritors members Karn and Morlun from the "Spider-Verse".666768 A snowy New York and a 2099 New York environments, as well as special Winter Holiday Events against Hydro-Man were implemented.6970 It added eight new Spider-Men from the "Spider-Verse" including Ultimate Jessica Drew, Miles Morales, Silk, and Spider-UK.7172 A new Spider-Woman's costume also debuted on the game before its comics debut in March 2015.73

In February 2015, the third update featuring the "Spider-Verse" storyline added eight new characters including Anya Corazon, Earth X Spider-Man, and Spider-Punk.74 The story mode was modified to accept the "Spidey Power"<U+2014>player's team multiplier<U+2014>rather than level to progress on it.75 It also included Daemos and Jennix to the "Great Hunt", a subway environment, and events with non-score-based leaderboards and unlimited revives.7677 Among the events is "Clash of the Spiders": to fight the Inheritors, Amazing Spider-Man and Superior Spider-Man create their own armies which ultimately starts a rivalry between them.78

At the Game Developers Conference 2015, Gameloft announced a new update to the game;79 in April 2015, it was released.80 The inclusion of Solus, father of the Inheritors, marks the last update for the "Spider-Verse" saga.81 Alongside, it features the return of the Sinister Six with the addition of a fifth issue to the story mode, featuring Doctor Octopus as the boss.82 New characters added includes Aaron Aikman, Clone Hunter Spider-Man, Superior Venom, and Spider-Ben.8384

Reception

Spider-Man Unlimited has been well-received by public, with more than 30 million downloads as of December 2014.85 It has received "generally favorable reviews" from critics, according to Metacritic, which assigns a score of 79 out of 100 based on ten reviews.86 Jim Squires, writing for GameZebo, declared that the game "is both how you do a runner right AND how you do a superhero game right."87 Polygon's Justin McElroy stated "Spider-Man Unlimited is a top-notch game all on its own."88 Shaun Musgrave of TouchArcade wrote "Spidey fans and runner fans should definitely check out Spider-Man Unlimited simply because it does so much right."89 Download.com staff wrote it "combines great graphics, super-spidey skills, and the old-school feel of a classic runner game into one entertaining package."90 For 148Apps.com, Jennifer Allen wrote, "Despite [some problems], Spider-Man Unlimited is a surprisingly well-made endless runner."91 Peter Willington of Pocket Gamer said, "If you come ... expecting an excellent Spider-Man game, you're going to be disappointed, as there's very little of Peter Parker in here. However, those looking for a high quality auto-runner, or fans of the genre keen for a change of scenery, will find a lot to like."92

Controls responsiveness was praised by Squires, Willington, and McElroy,939495 and its intuitiveness was remarked by Download.com staff.96 Musgrave and Willington praised voice acting, with the first also praising the music and the former, the sound effects;9798 animation was commended by Musgrave, Willington, and McElroy, with Musgrave and Willington commenting on the comic book-like style99100 and Musgrave and McElroy on the varied environments.101102 Squires said it has "a perfect mix of speed, humor, missions, and collectibles,"103 while McElroy and Download.com staff highlighted the possibility of having collectible Spider-Men.104105 The variability between running, swinging, climbing and freefalling was praised by Squires and Musgrave; the former stated, "The result is a game that continues to feel fresh long after the hundredth play.106

A problem found by Squires was the level cap limit when one is using only a character, which will "grate" some players.107 Musgrave affirmed "the art style does sometimes hinder the gameplay."108 Willington was critical of the story mode, which he argued had a "weak plot" and lacked character development.109 Download.com staff also noted some lags and bugs;110 Musgrave and Allen deemed the control as problematic sometimes to recognize swipes.111112 However the energy system was "the biggest point of controversy," as wrote Musgrave;113 he criticized it as well as did Willington, Allen, and Mike Fahey of Kotaku.114115116 On the other hand, McElroy noted the energy system is a "microtransaction hook" but that it is easily surmountable without spending any real money.117 Squires also pondered that because of the time a run lasts it "ends up feeling a lot fairer in practice than it sounds on paper."118
