Kloosterburen is a village in the Dutch province of Groningen. It is located in the municipality of De Marne.

Kloosterburen was a separate municipality until 1990, when it was merged with Leens, Ulrum and Eenrum. During the combining of the the 4 municipality they where called 'de LEUK gemeenten'. 1
