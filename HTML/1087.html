There are at least 280 species of non-marine molluscs living out of doors in Ukraine: 203 species of terrestrial molluscs1, more than 50 freshwater species of gastropods2 and 30 species of freshwater bivalvies3

Terrestrial molluscs

The list is given on the basis of "An annotated checklist of the terrestrial molluscs of Ukraine" published in Journal of Conchology in 20124. In this article 203 species are listed as registered in Ukraine.

Species that listed by the other sources are given with individual references.
