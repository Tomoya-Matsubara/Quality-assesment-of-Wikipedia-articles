Kevin Randolph Coble is a member of the Northwestern Wildcats basketball team. He led the Wildcats in scoring and rebounding as a freshman, sophomore and junior during the 2006-07, 2007-08 and 2008-09 seasons,123 but was injured and redshirtted during his true senior season. Following the 2008<U+2013>09 All-Big Ten Conference regular season, he was named a second-team All-conference selection by both the coaches and the media.4 He was also named Big Ten men's basketball player of the week during the season.5 He was an honorable mention All-Big Ten selection following the 2007<U+2013>08 Big Ten Conference men's basketball season.6
