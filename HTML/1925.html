Cori Bartel (born June 21, 19711 in Humboldt, Saskatchewan) is a Canadian curler from Calgary, Alberta. She plays lead for Cheryl Bernard.

Bartel grew up in Lanigan, Saskatchewan. In 1998, Bartel played in Ontario, and played in the provincial Scott Tournament of Hearts, as an alternate player for the Cheryl McBain team. She would later move to Alberta. Bartel joined Bernard in 2005 after playing for Heather Rankin. She has since been to the 2007 and 2009 Scotties Tournament of Hearts as a member of that team.

Cori and her team represented Canada in the 2010 Winter Olympics,2 and won silver medals at the tournament.3

Bartel is a graduate from the University of Ottawa and is employed as a professional recruiter.
