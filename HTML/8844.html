The Stone Mountain Provincial Park is an area of 256.91 square kilometres of mountain wilderness in the Canadian province of British Columbia. The park is part of the larger Muskwa-Kechika Management Area1 which includes the Northern Rocky Mountains Provincial Park, immediately south, and Kwadacha Wilderness Provincial Park. it and nearby Muncho Lake Provincial Park are accessed by the Alaska Highway, where it penetrates the Northern Rocky Mountains
