Festival Park is a shopping centre in Ebbw Vale, Wales. Festival Park is located on the former Garden Festival of Wales site. It was left abandoned after the closure of the festival in 1992, but has since then being converted into a shopping outlet and tourist attraction. It now includes an owl sanctuary, fishing lake, gym, the Festival Church and remaining sculptures from the Garden Festival of Wales.

Festival Park is especially popular with Christmas shoppers.
