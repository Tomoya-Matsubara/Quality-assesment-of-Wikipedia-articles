__NOTOC__ Gmina Lubycza Kr<U+00F3>lewska is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tomasz<U+00F3>w Lubelski County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland, on the border with Ukraine. Its seat is the village of Lubycza Kr<U+00F3>lewska.

The gmina covers an area of , and as of 2006 its total population is 7,115.

Neighbouring gminas

Gmina Lubycza Kr<U+00F3>lewska is bordered by the gminas of Be<U+0142><U+017C>ec, Horyniec-Zdr<U+00F3>j, Jarcz<U+00F3>w, Narol, Tomasz<U+00F3>w Lubelski and Ulh<U+00F3>wek. It also borders Ukraine.
