Kornele (German Kornellen) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Stary Dzierzgo<U+0144>, within Sztum County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.1 It lies approximately north-east of Stary Dzierzgo<U+0144>, east of Sztum, and south-east of the regional capital Gda<U+0144>sk.

Before 1945 the area was part of Germany. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.

The village has a population of 80.
