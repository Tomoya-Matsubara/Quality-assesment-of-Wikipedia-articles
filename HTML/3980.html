Viktor Sakhniuk (; born 4 June 1990 in Kiev) is a Ukrainian football forward who currently plays for FK Dukla Bansk<U+00E1> Bystrica.

FK Dukla Bansk<U+00E1> Bystrica

He made his full professional debut for FK Dukla Bansk<U+00E1> Bystrica on 16 September 2012 against M<U+0160>K <U+017D>ilina, the match ended 2 - 2 draw.
