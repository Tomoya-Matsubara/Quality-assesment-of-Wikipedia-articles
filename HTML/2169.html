Zilubostam (, also Romanized as Z<U+012B>l<U+016B>bos<U+1E6D><U+0101>m) is a village in Howmeh-ye Sarpol Rural District, in the Central District of Sarpol-e Zahab County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 48, in 9 families.1
