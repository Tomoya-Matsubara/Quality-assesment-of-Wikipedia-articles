21962 Scottsandford (provisional designation: 1999 VS203) is a main-belt minor planet. It was discovered through the Lowell Observatory Near-Earth-Object Search at the Anderson Mesa Station in Coconino County, Arizona, on November 9, 1999. It is named after Scott Alan Sandford, a researcher at NASA's Ames Research Center.
