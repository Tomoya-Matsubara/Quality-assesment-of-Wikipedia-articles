Chevry-en-Sereine is a French commune located in the Seine-et-Marne d<U+00E9>partement, in the <U+00CE>le-de-France r<U+00E9>gion.

Demographics

The inhabitants are called Chevriots.

As of the census of 1999, the village has a population of 454.
