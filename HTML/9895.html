Jamie Schaafsma (born February 11, 1983) is a Canadian-born professional ice hockey player. He has played in several international tournaments as a member of the Netherlands men's national ice hockey team. Schaafsma is currently playing with the Allen Americans of the Central Hockey League (CHL).

On February 4, 2013, Schaafsma made his American Hockey League debut playing with the Houston Aeros.1
