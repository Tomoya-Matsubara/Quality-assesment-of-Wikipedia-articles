The 2013–14 season will be Queen of the South's first season back in the second tier of Scottish football and the first in the newly established Scottish Championship,1 having been promoted as champions from the Scottish Second Division at the end of the 2012–13 season.2 Queens will also compete in the Challenge Cup, League Cup and the Scottish Cup.

Summary

Management

The club were managed by Jim McIntyre for the 2013–14 season,3 following the departures of Allan Johnston and Sandy Clark who left to become the new manager and assistant of Scottish Premiership side Kilmarnock.45
