Mashak-e Tehranchi (, also Romanized as M<U+0101>shak-e Tehr<U+0101>nch<U+012B>; also known as M<U+0101>shak)1 is a village in Kurka Rural District, in the Central District of Astaneh-ye Ashrafiyeh County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,018, in 296 families.2
