8 is the eighth full-length studio album by Canadian/Indonesian avant-garde metal band Kekal, announced on June 23, 2010,1 and made available for purchase on January 23, 2011.2 While the band has no officially active members, former members Jeff, Leo, and Levi all contributed to the album.345 The album was set to be released by Whirlwind Records during December, 2010, on CD and as a digital download,6 and on December 15, 2010, the album was available for pre-order, which included an offer of free shipping within Europe up to December 24.7

On February 19, 2010, Jeff announced that he had posted a music video on Youtube 1 for a song entitled "Tabula Rasa". At that time, the new album was still untitled.89 On March 24 2011, it was announced that Tabula Rasa was included on a compilation/sampler album by Sonic Seducer Magazine.1011
