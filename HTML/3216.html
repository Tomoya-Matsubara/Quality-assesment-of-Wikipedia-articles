Tatum Gressette (March 22, 1900 – July 19, 1997)1 was the ninth head college football coach for the The Citadel Bulldogs located in Charleston, South Carolina and he held that position for eight seasons, from 1932 until 1939. His career coaching record at The Citadel was 34 wins, 41 losses, and 3 ties. This ranks him fifth at The Citadel in total wins and 15th at The Citadel in winning percentage.2
