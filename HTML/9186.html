Marquez Haynes (born December 19, 1986) is an American professional basketball player for Emporio Armani Milano of the Italian League.

A native of Irving, Texas, he played college basketball at Boston College (2005<U+2013>2007) and Texas<U+2013>Arlington (2008<U+2013>2010). After going undrafted in the 2010 NBA draft, Haynes decided to pursue a professional basketball career in Europe, signing with <U+00C9>lan Chalon of the French LNB Pro A.1 He later played for CB Gran Canaria (2011<U+2013>12), the Artland Dragons (2012<U+2013>13), and has recently signed with Olimpia Milano.2
