"You and I" is a song recorded by International Supermodel Ayyan for her First studio album, Nothing Like Everything (2014). Featuring Romanian rapper F .Charm (rapper), it was released by Red Clover Media on Augest 1, 2014 as the lead single from the album. "You and I" was written by Ayyan, F .Charm, along with the song's producer Ayyan. It is an up-tempo electronic dance
