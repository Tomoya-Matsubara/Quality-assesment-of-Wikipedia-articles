Blues for Smoke is an album by pianist Jaki Byard recorded in 1960 and released on the Candid label.1

Reception

Allmusic awarded the album 4<U+00BD> stars with its review by Scott Yanow stating, "A highly recommended outing from a very underrated pianist".2
