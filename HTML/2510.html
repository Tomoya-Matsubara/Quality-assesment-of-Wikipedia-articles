Mazi Kalleh (, also Romanized as M<U+0101>z<U+012B> Kalleh; also known as Muzikalah)1 is a village in Ahandan Rural District, in the Central District of Lahijan County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 131, in 34 families.2
