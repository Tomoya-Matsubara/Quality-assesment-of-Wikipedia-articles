Yudh is an upcoming Indian television mini series directed by Anurag Kashyap starring actor Amitabh Bachchan in the lead role. 12 It also stars veteran actors like Nawazuddin Siddiqui, Sarika and Kay Kay Menon.3 This show is Amitabh Bachchan's debut fiction television show .45 Produced by Endemol India and Saraswati Creations, Yudh is scheduled to premier on Sony Entertainment Television India on 14 July 2014.6 Yudh replaced the soap opera Bade Achhe Lagte Hain, produced by the Balaji Telefilms, which had been on air since 2011.7

Plot

Yudhisthir Sikhawar, an elite businessman has a neuropsychological disorder. He is now caught in a battle with himself and his health, an emotional battle with his family and a corporate battle with his rivals.
