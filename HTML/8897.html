Cui Xiaodi (,1 born March 7, 1989) is a Chinese ski mountaineer, and member of the national selection of the People's Republic of China.

Selected results

  • 2007:
    • 4th, Asian Championship2
  • 2009:
    • 2nd, Asian Championship, relay (mixed teams), together with Huang Chunsen, Yin Yubo and Xin Detao34
    • 3rd, Asian Championship, individual5
    • 3rd, Asian Championship, vertical race67
