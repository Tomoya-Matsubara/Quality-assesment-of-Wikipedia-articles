Acharya Institute of Technology (AIT, ) is a private co-educational engineering and management college in Bangalore, India. AIT is affiliated with the Visvesvaraya Technological University and is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. Established in 2000, it offers eleven courses at undergraduate level and six courses at postgraduate level.1 AIT includes the NRV School of Architecture, which offers a five-year undergraduate course in architecture and is accredited by the Council of Architecture (COA).23 The college has various collaborations and tie-ups with various industries and universities across the world.4

It is one of the several institutes run by the JMJ Education Society5 and features in various rankings lists of top engineering colleges in India published by media agencies and publications like Outlook,6 CSR-GHRDC,7 Dataquest,8910 NASSCOM and Silicon India,11 EFY.12 In 2011 rankings, AIT was ranked among the top five Engineering colleges in Karnataka by the Global Human Resources Development Centre (GHRDC).13 It features as an A+ institution in the rankings of the publication Career 360.14 The college was also conferred with South India's Excellent Institution Award by A-Plus TV Media Works.15

AIT organises an annual week-long inter-collegiate cultural fest known as the Acharya Habba, which is one of the largest in Bangalore and Karnataka.161718

The postgraduate courses in the institute, especially MBA, makes it one of most notable colleges in the Karnataka Management Aptitude Test (KMAT).19

History

The Acharya Institute of Technology (AIT) was established in 2000 by Shri Premnath Reddy, chairman of Acharya Group of Institutions.20 The college is managed by the JMJ Education Society, headquartered in Bangalore.21 AIT offers the Bachelor of Engineering course in eleven disciplines, and Master of Technology in four disciplines: Digital Communication Engineering and Biotechnology, Computer Science and Engineering, Machine Design, MBA and MCA. All courses are affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU).22

It was the first college under the VTU to start an undergraduate course in Mechatronics Engineering in Karnataka.2324

In 2009, the college constructed a long road from its campus to the Hessarghatta Main Road. The road was inaugurated by Sree Sree Shivakumara Swamiji of Siddaganga Math in September 2010. The road is named after former Indian President Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan and is known as Acharya Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan Road.2526

Academic profile

Admission criteria

AIT admits students on basis of their Karnataka CET or their COMEDK UGET ranks.27 AIT also admits students under Management quota, which does not place any merit requirements.

Yearly, AIT admits over 150 foreign nationals from 25 countries under the PIO Quota.2829

There is a lateral entry scheme in place, by which students holding diploma degrees can enter directly to the second year of study In engineering. Students, after completing their graduation, receive a B.E. degree from the VTU.

Students are admitted to postgraduate courses on basis of their GATE test scores, as well as on their Karnataka Post Graduate CET (PGCET) and KMAT PGCET scores.30

Accredition and Rankings

AIT is one of the few colleges in Karnataka to be accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).43 It was the first college under the Visvesvaraya Technological University to start an undergraduate course in Mechatronics Engineering in Karnataka.44 It is one of the seven colleges in Karnataka to be funded by the World Bank and is granted endowments by the Visvesvaraya Technological University to carry out research works in both its undergraduate and postgraduate departments.4546

The college is ranked consistently among the top 50 Engineering colleges in India by publications and organizations namely Outlook India,4748 Data Quest,495051 NASSCOM and Silicon India,52 EFY,53 CSR-GHRDC5455 and CSR.56 AIT was ranked among the top 5 Engineering colleges in Karnataka by the Global Human Resources Development Centre(GHRDC).57 It was also bestowed South India's Excellent Institution Award by A-Plus TV Media Works.58

AIT features as an A+ institution in the rankings of the publication Career 360.59

Administration

Governing council

The governing council of the college consists of 11 members. The body comprises five members from the JMJ Education Society, one staff representative, one University Grants Commission member, one AICTE nominee, one state government, Dept. of Technical Education nominee, one university nominee, and one Member Secretary, the principal.6061

Academic council

The academic council of the college is headed by the principal. Student enrollments, academics, assessments, examinations are managed by the respective deans, controller of examinations and appointed faculty members. The college administration is managed by the director of administration. The research department is headed by the director of R&D. The placement department is managed by the principal, director of HR and one expert in the subject. Each academic department is headed by respective heads of departments (HoDs).6263

Proctorial system

Faculty members are appointed as proctors for the students of their respective departments to monitor their attendance, performance in tests, co-curricular activities and personality development. Each proctor supervises 20 students in an academic semester.6465

Infrastructure

The college provides a 24/7 campus Wi-Fi access.74 The college has an Auditorium with a capacity of 500+ seats, Board rooms, Conference and Seminar Halls. The institution has its own multipurpose stadium known as the Smt. Nagarathnama Stadium with a capacity of over 10,000 people.75 The college also has a National Technical Manpower Information System (NTMIS)76 and 24/7 ATM center/outlet operated by Axis Bank. The college operates a Campus clinic which provides medical facilities to the students.

Environmental Initiatives

The institution has built its own sewage treatment plant in the campus and has employed methods for rain water harvesting. The campus also boasts of a four-acre artificial lake.77

Hostels and Canteens

AIT provides Hostel Accommodation for both boys and girls. The Hostel Mess serves North Indian, South Indian and Chinese cuisine. The college has separate Food Court, Cafeteria and Canteen which serve vegetarian food in the campus. The campus also houses a Coffee Day Xpress operated by the Coffee Day.78

The college has a Student activity center which provides indoor games and gymnasium facilities. The institution also organises horse riding and yoga learning camps on campus for the students.7980

Student projects

Hovercraft

An one-seater hovercraft was designed by a team of Mechanical Engineering students of AIT as their final year project work. The hovercraft measured 8.5 feet in length and runs on a Yezdi Roadking 250cc, 16 bhp, 2-stroke engine powered by Race Engine Technology. The engine can accommodate high load operating conditions and has been designed for low speed operations. The weighing capability of the craft is 260 kilograms. The hovercraft is also equipped with GPS and has been made cost efficient.96
