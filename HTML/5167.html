Reidar Andreas Lyseth (born 14 July 1904 in Trondheim, died 1 March 1987) was a Norwegian politician for the Labour Party.

He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from the Market towns of S<U+00F8>r-Tr<U+00F8>ndelag and Nord-Tr<U+00F8>ndelag in 1950, and was re-elected on one occasion. He had previously served in the position of deputy representative during the term 1945–1949.

Lyseth was a member of Trondheim city council in 1931–1934, and a member of its executive committee in the periods 1945–1947, 1947–1951 and 1951–1954.
