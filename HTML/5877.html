Edson Bindilatti (born March 13, 1979 in Bahia, Brazil1) is a decathlete and bobsledder from Brazil. He competed for Brazil at the 2014 Winter Olympics in the four-man competition2 where he placed in 29th position out of 30 teams along with (F<U+00E1>bio Gon<U+00E7>alves Silva, Edson Martins and Odirlei Pessoni).3 He also competed for Brazil at the 2002 and 2006 Winter Olympics.
