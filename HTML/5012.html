The Flora School, at 82744 Church St. in Flora, Oregon, is a historic Bungalow/craftsman-style school that was built in 1915. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997.1 It was deemed significant for association with education and the community, and for its architecture. It is a building that, at the time of its 1996 NRHP nomination, had been vacant since 1977 and then had a weathered condition.2
