System Capital Management or SCM () is a Ukrainian company that belongs to local tycoon Rinat Akhmetov and controls his stakes in companies in the country and around the world. Particularly, SCM controls large steel and mining (both iron ore and coal) companies in Eastern Ukraine.

According to an article in Invest-Gazeta on 13/01/04 entitled "A house that is becoming crowded", it is suggested that of the Donetsk Finiancial-Industrial Groups (FIGS) that System Capital Management (reportedly controlled by Akhmetov) has effectively become the dominant Donetsk FIG by early 2004, is expanding within the region and is expanding both within Ukraine and internationally.

The following assets as under control of SCM:1

  • Kerch integrated iron and steel works
  • Yenakiyeve iron and steel works
  • Azovstal integrated iron and steel works
  • Druzhkivka ore directorate
  • Silur open joint-stock company
  • Central ore-processing plant
  • Khartsyzk tube and pipe works
  • Markokhim open joint-stock company
  • Azovmash open joint-stock company
  • Rogan brewery
  • Shakhtar Donetsk Football Club
  • TV & Radio Company Ukraina (TRK Ukraina)
  • Donbass Palace Hotel (5-star hotel in Donetsk, Ukraine)
