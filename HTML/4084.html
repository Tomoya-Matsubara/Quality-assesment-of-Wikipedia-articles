Bruna Beatriz Benites Soares, or simply Bruna, (born October 16, 1985) is a Brazilian footballer. She plays as a defender for S<U+00E3>o Jos<U+00E9> and the Brazil women's national football team.

Personal life

She is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.1 She is also has a degree in Physiotherapy having studied at Universidade Catolica Dom Bosco in Campo Grande.2

National Team

Bruna made her debut at the 2012 Summer Olympics. She received a yellow card against Great Britain and in the Quarter Final defeat to Japan.3
