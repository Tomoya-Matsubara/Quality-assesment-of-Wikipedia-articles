Sahmir Gar<U+010D>evi<U+0107> (born May 11, 1973) is a retired Bosnian football player.

He played as forward or Attacking midfielder. His playing career include clubs such as FK Budu<U+0107>nost Podgorica, FK Obili<U+0107>, FK Beograd, FK <U+017D>eljezni<U+010D>ar Sarajevo, NK <U+017D>ep<U+010D>e and FK Baskimi.

Honours

  • Obili<U+0107> Belgrade
    • First League of FR Yugoslavia: 1997-98
  • <U+017D>eljezni<U+010D>ar Sarajevo
    • Premier League of Bosnia and Herzegovina: 2001-02
