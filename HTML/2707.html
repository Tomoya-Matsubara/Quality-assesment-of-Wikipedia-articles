Bab Duri (, also Romanized as B<U+0101>b D<U+016B>r<U+012B>)1 is a village in Dasht-e Khak Rural District, in the Central District of Zarand County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 106, in 26 families.2
