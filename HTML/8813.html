Al-Watan Al-Akbar (, translated The Greatest Homeland) was an Arab Nationalist song composed by the Egyptian Mohammed Abdel Wahab in 1960.

History

It was a Pan Arabist nationalist song was composed to celebrate the union of Egypt and Syria into the United Arab Republic.1234
