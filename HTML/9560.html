Budk<U+00F3>w is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wi<U+0144>sko, within Wo<U+0142><U+00F3>w County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany.

It lies approximately west of Wi<U+0144>sko, north-west of Wo<U+0142><U+00F3>w, and north-west of the regional capital Wroc<U+0142>aw.

The village has a population of 154.
