Anash and the Legacy of the Sun-Rock is a half hour children<U+2019>s show first produced for the Aboriginal Peoples Television Network, an aboriginal television network in Canada. A mid-1800s, animated children's show based on Tlingit cultural stories about maintaining principles.1
