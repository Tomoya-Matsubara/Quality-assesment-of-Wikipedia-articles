Yasnogorsk () is a town in Tula Oblast, Russia, located on the Vashana River (Oka's tributary), 35 km north of Tula. Population: 18,588 (2002 Census); 21,292 (1989 Census).

The village of Laptevo () has been known since the 15th–16th centuries. It received town status and renamed Laptev () in 1958. It was renamed Yasnogorsk in 1965.
