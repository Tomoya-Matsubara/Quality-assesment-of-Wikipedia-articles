CIVO United Football Club (CIVO Utd) is a Malawian football (soccer) club based in Lilongwe and currently plays in the TNM Super League the top division of Malawian football. 1

In 1987 the team has won the TNM Super League.

Stadium

Currently the team plays at the Civo Stadium.
