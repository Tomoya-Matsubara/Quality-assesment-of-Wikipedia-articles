Umar Shaikh Mirza II (1469 - 1492 C.E.) was the ruler of the Fergana Valley. He was the son of Abu Sa'id Mirza, the ruler of Timurid Empire in what is now Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, eastern Iran and Afghanistan. He married Qutlugh Nigar Khanum, the daughter of Yunus Khan of Moghulistan and had issue three sons; Babur Mirza, Jahangir Mirza II and Nasir Mirza. His eldest son Babur founded the Mughal Empire in 1525 and was the first Mughal Emperor of India.
