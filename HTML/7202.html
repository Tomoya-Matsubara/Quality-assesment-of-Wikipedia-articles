Hapoel Hod HaSharon () is an Israeli football club based in Hod HaSharon. They are currently in Liga Alef South division.

History

The club was founded in 1965.1 A Previous club, Hapoel Hadar Ramatayim, which represented the settlements that merged to create Hod HaSharon in 1964, was active until the 1962<U+2013>63 season.

Hapoel Hod HaSharon reached Liga Bet for the first time in 1975<U+2013>76 season, where they finished second bottom in Liga Bet South A division, and relegated back to Liga Gimel, where they played, until promoted back to Liga Bet in the 1990s. in the 2008<U+2013>09 season the club finished second bottom in Liga Bet South A division, and relegated to Liga Gimel, where they spent two seasons, until they finished runners-up in the 2010<U+2013>11 season in Liga Gimel Sharon division,2 and promoted back to Liga Bet. In the 2013<U+2013>14 season, the club finished runners-up in Liga Bet South A division,3 and qualified to the promotion play-offs, and after beating Hapoel Bik'at HaYarden, Maccabi Ironi Kfar Yona, and Hapoel Rahat, faced Maccabi Be'er Sheva in the decisive promotion/relegation play-offs. Hapoel Hod HaSharon won 1-0, with a goal by Itay Menachem in the 89th minute,4 and promoted to Liga Alef.
