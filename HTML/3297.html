Bid Barzeh-ye Sefidar (, also Romanized as B<U+012B>d Barzeh-e Sef<U+012B>d<U+0101>r; also known as B<U+012B>d Barzeh)1 is a village in Sepidar Rural District, in the Central District of Boyer-Ahmad County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 100, in 20 families.2
