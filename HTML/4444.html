The Salfit Governorate () is one of 16 Governorates of the Palestinian National Authority. It is located in the northwestern West Bank, bordered by the governorates of Ramallah and al-Bireh to the south, Nablus to the east and Qalqilya in the north as well as, Israel to the west. Its district capital and largest city is Salfit. According to the Palestinian Central Bureau of Statistics (PCBS), the governorate had a population of 64,129 inhabitants in mid-year 2006.1 In the PCBS's census in 1997, Palestinian refugee|refugees accounted for 7.7% of the total population.2 There were 23,758 males and 22,913 females.3
