The Roman Catholic Diocese of Blumenau () is a diocese located in the city of Blumenau in the Ecclesiastical province of Florian<U+00F3>polis in Brazil.

History

  • April 19, 2000: Established as Diocese of Blumenau from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Florian<U+00F3>polis, Diocese of Joinville and Diocese of Rio do Sul

Leadership

  • Bishops of Blumenau (Roman rite)
    • Bishop Ang<U+00E9>lico S<U+00E2>ndalo Bernardino (April 19, 2000 <U+2013> present)
