John Baldwin Munro, QSO, JP (born 1936), better known as J. B. Munro1 was a New Zealand politician of the Labour Party.

He was raised as a foster child and at the age of nine was adopted into the Munro family.2 His adoptive brother was Burt Munro, the New Zealand motorcycle racer of The World's Fastest Indian fame.

He represented the electorate in Parliament from to 1975, when he was defeated by Norman Jones.3
