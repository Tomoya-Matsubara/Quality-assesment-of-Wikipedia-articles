Dr Janina Ramirez (born 1973) is a British historian. lecturer and TV presenter.1

Dr Janina Ramirez gained a degree in English literature (specializing in Old and Middle English) from St Anne's College, Oxford, before completed a post-graduate studies at the Centre for Medieval Studies, York. She completed a art/literature PhD on the symbolism of birds, which led to a lectureship in York<U+2019>s Art History department, followed by lecturing posts at Winchester, Warwick, and Oxford.
