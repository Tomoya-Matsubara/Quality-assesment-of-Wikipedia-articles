Bath High School is a public high school located near Lima, Ohio. It is the only high school in the Bath Local School District.

Organizations

School organizations at Bath include: Band, Symphonic Band, Choir, FCA, National Honors Society, Quiz Bowl, Science Fair, Math Counts, SADD, Science Olympiad, Student Council, and Yearbook editors.1

Athletics

The school's mascot is a wildcat for boys' sports, and a wildkitten for girls'. They are members of the Western Buckeye League.

Recognition

U.S. News & World Report awarded Bath High School a bronze award for their America's Best High Schools 2009 rankings.14 They were the only high school in Allen County to be recognized.15
