Huang Chong (<U+9EC3><U+5D07>) was an officer of the Three Kingdoms Period of China, and was the son of Huang Quan.

Despite his father's surrender to Wei, Chong remained with the Kingdom of Shu. Following the time in which Deng Ai invaded Shu, Chong defended the Mian Zhu Gate with Zhuge Zhan. However, Chong ended up dying in battle.
