Lata Mondal () (born: 16 January 1993, Dhaka) is a Bangladeshi cricketer who plays for the Bangladesh national women's cricket team.12 She is a right handed batsman.

Early life and background

Lata was born on January 16, 1993 in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Career

ODI career

Lata made her ODI career against Ireland women's cricket team on November 26, 2011.

T20I career

Lata also made her T20I career against Ireland women's cricket team on August 28, 2012.

Asian games

Bangladesh women team won historic Silver medal in Asian games Women cricket competition in 2010 against China national women's cricket team. Farzana was part of the team at the Asian Games in Guangzhou, China.34
