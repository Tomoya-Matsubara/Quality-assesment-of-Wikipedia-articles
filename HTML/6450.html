Crissy Ahmann-Leighton (born 20 May 1970) is an American swimmer and olympic champion. She competed at the 1992 Olympic Games in Barcelona, where she received a gold medal in 4 × 100 m medley relay, a gold medal in 4 × 100 m freestyle relay, and a silver medal in 100 m butterfly.1
