Mergers

  • In 2009 Montfavergier merged into Montfaucon and Le Peuchapatte merged into Muriaux. At the same time, Goumois and Les Pommerats merged into Saignel<U+00E9>gier. Also in 2009, Epauvillers and Epiquerez merged into Clos du Doubs of the Porrentruy District.2
