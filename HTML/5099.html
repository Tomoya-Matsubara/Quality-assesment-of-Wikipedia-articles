Wayne Stevens (born June 19, 1936) is an American former professional basketball player.1 Stevens was selected in the 1958 NBA Draft by the Cincinnati Royals after a collegiate career at the University of Cincinnati.2 He played for the Royals in just eight games during the beginning of the 1959<U+2013>60 NBA season and averaged 1.6 points, 2.0 rebounds and 0.5 assists per game.3
