Chalgarud (, also Romanized as Ch<U+0101>lgar<U+016B>d; also known as Ch<U+0101>lgar)1 is a village in Sanjabad-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Kowsar County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 531, in 110 families.2
