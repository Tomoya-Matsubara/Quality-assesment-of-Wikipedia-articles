Peter Hartung (born 1959) is the director of the Adelaide Institute having previously been a successful businessman and political adviser. He is a native of Adelaide, Australia.

Hartung assumed the role of director of the Institute, following the incarceration of Dr Gerald Fredrick T<U+00F6>ben at Mannheim Prison for breaching Germany's Holocaust Law, Section 130, that prohibits anyone from "defaming the dead".12
