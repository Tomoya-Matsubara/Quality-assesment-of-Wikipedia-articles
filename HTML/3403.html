' Andanoor' () is a village in the Gandaravakottai revenue block of Pudukkottai district,1 Tamil Nadu, India.

Demographics

As per the 2001 census, Andanoor had a total population of 24232 with 1214 males and 1209 females. Out of the total population 1146 people were literate.
