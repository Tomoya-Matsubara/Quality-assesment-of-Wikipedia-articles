Rangin Ban (, also Romanized as Rang<U+012B>n B<U+0101>n and Rang<U+012B>n Bon; also known as Rang<U+012B>n B<U+0101>n-e B<U+0101>zvand)1 is a village in Rumeshkhan-e Sharqi Rural District, Rumeshkhan District, Kuhdasht County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,551, in 324 families.2
