Steffan Browning (born 3 July 1954) is a New Zealand politician and a member of the New Zealand House of Representatives. He is a member of the Green Party of Aotearoa New Zealand.

Early life

Browning was born in Clyde, New Zealand in 1954.1 He has been employed by the Soil & Health Association as its spokesperson since 2003.23

Member of Parliament

Browning is a member of the Green Party of Aotearoa New Zealand. He was ranked 19th on their party list at the , eleventh in 2005 and twelfth in 2008. He stood as the Greens candidate in the Kaik<U+014D>ura electorate.

Browning was placed in tenth place on the Green Party list for the .,4 when he was elected to Parliament, with the Greens gaining 14 seats. For the ,5 he was demoted to number 15.
