Doris Maletzki (born June 11, 1952 in Salzwedel) is a retired East German sprinter who specialized in the 200 metres.

She won a gold medal in 4 x 100 metres relay at the 1974 European Championships, together with teammates Christina Heinich, B<U+00E4>rbel Eckert and Renate Stecher. At the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal she won a bronze medal in same event, with her teammates Brigitte Rohde, 400 m silver medalist Christina Brehmer and 400 m bronze medalist Ellen Streidt.

She competed for the club SC Dynamo Berlin during her active career.
