Amelia Frid (born 30 July, 1975 in Russia) is an actress who played Cody Willis in Neighbours. She retired from acting to concentrate on studies, current profession: forensic psychology. Amelia's final appearance as Cody Willis and final acting work was July 30, 1991 which coincidentally was her 16th birthday. She is of Jewish faith and of Australian nationality.

Other credits

  • Celia (1987)
  • Neighbours (1989-1991)
  • Adventures on Kythera (1990)
