Patrick J Brady (born Dublin March 11 1936) was an Irish footballer who played in England in the late 1950's and early 1960's with Millwall and QPR.

Ray signed by Alec Stock for QPR from Millwall in 1963 and made his debut in October of that year against Hull City. In all Pat made 62 league appearances for QPR.

His brothers Ray Brady and more famously Liam Brady were also professional footballers.
