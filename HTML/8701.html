This list of tallest buildings in Seattle ranks skyscrapers in the city of Seattle, Washington by height. The tallest building in Seattle is currently the 76-story Columbia Center, which rises 937 feet (285 meters). It is currently the 18th-tallest building in the United States, and the tallest building in the state of Washington. Another famous Seattle skyscraper is the Washington Mutual Tower, which stands as the 2nd-tallest building in the city and the state.

Tallest buildings

This list ranks Seattle skyscrapers that stand at least 400 feet (122 meters) tall, based on standard height measurement. This includes spires and architectural details but does not include antenna masts. Existing structures are included for ranking purposes based on present height.
