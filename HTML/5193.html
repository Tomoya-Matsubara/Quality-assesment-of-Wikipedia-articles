Chlamys is a genus of small scallops, marine bivalve molluscs in the family Pectinidae. The name is taken from the Ancient Greek, <U+03C7><U+03BB><U+03B1><U+03BC><U+03B0><U+03C2> or Chlamys, a cloak worn by soldiers.
