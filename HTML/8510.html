Paul Falkenberg (; 1848 - 1925) - German botanist; Professor of botany and Director of Botanical Garden Rostock; renowed for, mainly, his works in anatomy and morphology of plants (<U+201C>Vergleichende Untersuchungen der Vegetationsorgane der Monocotyledonen<U+201D>, Stuttgart, 1876), also, on the algae. Falkenberg dedicated much on the study of the most extensive and most difficult group red algae Rhodomelaceae (<U+201C>Rhodomelaceae<U+201D> in Engler und Prantl's Die nat<U+00FC>rlichen Pflanzenfamilien, Leipzig., 1897).
