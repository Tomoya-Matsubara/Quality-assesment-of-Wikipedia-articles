Lee Min-Kyu (; born 6 January 1989) is a South Korean football Defender, who plays for Gangwon FC in the K-League.

Club career

After completing his university education at Hongik University, Lee was placed on the 2011 intake of the K-League draft. Joining Gangwon FC as their fourth choice pick, his first game for his new club was as a starter in the second round match of the 2011 K-League Cup against the Chunnam Dragons.1 Four days later on 10 April, Lee made his debut in the K-League itself, playing in Gangwon's 1 - 0 loss to Ulsan Hyundai.2
