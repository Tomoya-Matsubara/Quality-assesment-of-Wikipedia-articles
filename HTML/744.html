The Ponale is a long river originating in Lake Ledro (Lago di Ledro).

The river first passes Molina di Ledro, Pr<U+00E8> di Ledro and Biacesa, there feeding a fish farm. Starting from Biacesa the Ponale enters a steep canyon and after a cascade the Ponale flows into Lake Garda close to Porto del Ponale.
