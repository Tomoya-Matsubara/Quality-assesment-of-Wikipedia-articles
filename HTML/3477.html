Get Back - Together is the second album by the reformed Liverpool band The Quarrymen, which was the band that, in its original conception, evolved into The Beatles. The album was recorded in Liverpool in 1997 and released the same year. The content of the album is drawn from the early repertoire of the original Quarrymen (which included John Lennon, Paul McCartney and George Harrison) and features fifteen songs that were regularly performed live by The Quarrymen in the late fifties. The album's title refers to The Beatles song "Get Back".

Track listing

  1. Mean Woman Blues
  2. Midnight Special (Traditional)
    • Covered by The Beatles in 1969 during their Get Back Sessions. Also covered by Paul McCartney on his live Unplugged album as well as a regular part of Paul McCartney's sound check set.
  3. Blue Moon Of Kentucky (Bill Monroe)
    • Covered by The Beatles in 1994 during the reunion sessions for the Beatles Anthology project. Also covered by Paul McCartney on his live Unplugged album.
  4. I Forgot To Remember To Forget
    • Covered live by The Beatles on BBC radio and released on disc in 1994.
  5. Whole Lotta Shakin' Goin' On (Jerry Lee Lewis)
  6. Pick A Bale Of Cotton
  7. That's All Right (Mama)
    • Covered live by The Beatles on BBC radio and released on disc in 1994.
  8. Blue Suede Shoes (Carl Perkins)
    • The Beatles recorded an impromptu version of this song during their Get Back Sessions in January of 1969. An edited version of that performance was released on The Beatles Anthology 3 album in 1995.
  9. When The Sun Goes Down
  10. Don't Be Cruel
  11. You're Right, I'm Left, She's Gone
  12. Be-Bop-A-Lula (Gene Vincent / Tex Davis)
    • Covered by John Lennon on his 1975 Rock 'N' Roll LP.
  13. Have I Told You Lately That I Love You?
  14. Twenty Flight Rock (Eddie Cochran)
    • John Lennon was impressed with Paul McCartney when he showed him how to play this song in 1957.
  15. Lost John
