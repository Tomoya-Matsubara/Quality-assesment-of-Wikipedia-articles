The Sylhet Oakblue (Amblypodia silhetensis)1 is a small butterfly found in India that belongs to the Lycaenids or Blues family.

Range

The butterfly occurs in India from Sikkim onto north Myanmar, and, from Karens to south Myanmar. 2

Status

Rare.3
