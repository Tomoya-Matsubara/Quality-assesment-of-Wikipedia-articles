Richard Mulligan is a former football player who represented New Zealand.

Mulligan made his full All Whites debut in a 5-0 win over Fiji on 3 June, 19851 and ended his international playing career with 24 A-international caps,2 his final cap being in a 0-1 loss, also against Fiji, on 19 November, 1988.3

Mulligan is still involved with football, currently as manager of Gisborne City.4
