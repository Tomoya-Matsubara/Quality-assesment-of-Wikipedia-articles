Sigurd Lersbryggen (born 8 July 1901 in Str<U+00F8>mm, died 2 February 1980) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party.

He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Vestfold in 1945, and was re-elected on two occasions.

Lersbryggen served as mayor of Str<U+00F8>mm municipality in the periods 1934–1937, 1937–1940 and 1945, and as a regular municipality council member in 1931–1934, 1940–1941 and 1955–1959.
