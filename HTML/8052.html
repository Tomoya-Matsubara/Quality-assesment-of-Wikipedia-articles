Zayegan (, also Romanized as Z<U+0101>yeg<U+0101>n and Z<U+0101>yg<U+0101>n)1 is a village in Rudbar-e Qasran Rural District, Rudbar-e Qasran District, Shemiranat County, Tehran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 425, in 130 families.2
