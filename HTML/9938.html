Ian Miller (born 13 May 1955) is a former Australian footballer who played his football in England.

Playing career

Miller played for Bury, Nottingham Forest, Doncaster Rovers, Swindon Town and Blackburn Rovers- where he was a member of the side that won the Full Members Cup in 1987. He joined Port Vale in June 1989. He began the 1989-90 season as a regular, but lost his place by December 1989 and was released in August 1990. He moved on to Scunthorpe United and later Stafford Rangers.

Coaching career

Miller remained active in the game after retiring as a player. He returned to Port Vale as the Community Programme leader in August 1991 and became the youth development officer in May 1992. In November 1994 he left the Vale to become the reserve team coach at Wolverhampton Wanderers.1 He later coached at Blackburn before joining the staff at Leeds United in 2008.2
