The 1983 World Junior Ice Hockey Championships (1983 WJHC) was the 7th edition of the World Junior Ice Hockey Championships and was held in Leningrad, Soviet Union between December 26, 1982 and January 4, 1983. The host Soviet team won the tournament with a perfect 7<U+2013>0 record.

Tournament results

The tournament was a round-robin format, with each team playing each of the other seven teams once each.
