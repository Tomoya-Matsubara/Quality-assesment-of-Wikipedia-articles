Bogachiel State Park in Clallam County, Washington, United States is a State Park. It is located approximately six miles (9.7 km) south of the city of Forks on Highway 101.1 Bogachiel was established in 1931 and the state began staffing it in 1961.2

The park consists of of thick forest on the banks of the Bogachiel River. Available activities include camping, hiking, bird watching, and wildlife viewing. It often also serves as a base camp for steelhead anglers and other winter recreators.3
