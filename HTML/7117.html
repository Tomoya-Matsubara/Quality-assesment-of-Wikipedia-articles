Dominic Chambers (born January 6, 1984) is an English cricketer. He is a right-handed batsman and a left-arm slow bowler who played for Kent during the 2006 season, and remains a squad member for 2007. He was born in Canterbury.

Chambers, who had made five Second XI appearances between May and August 2003, as well as representing Kent in the Second XI trophy during the same year, having turned in several impressive performances for Folkestone Cricket Club during 2002, appeared for Kent during a 2006 University tour by Cambridge University. Chambers made a modest contribution with the bat, while not contributing with the ball.

Chambers was an upper-middle order batsman.
