Hasanabad (, also Romanized as <U+1E28>asan<U+0101>b<U+0101>d)1 is a village in Gavork-e Nalin Rural District, Vazineh District, Sardasht County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 65, in 11 families.2
