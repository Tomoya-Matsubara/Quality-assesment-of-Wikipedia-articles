Harold ("Harry") Cartwright (born 12 May 1951 at Half Way Houses, Derbyshire) is a former English cricketer active from 1971 to 1979 who played for Derbyshire. He appeared in 82 first-class matches as a righthanded batsman, scoring 2,384 runs with a highest score of 141* and held 31 catches. He occasionally played as a wicketkeeper.1
