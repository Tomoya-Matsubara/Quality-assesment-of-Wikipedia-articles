Cody Gibson (born September 11, 1987) is an American mixed martial artist who competes as a bantamweight in the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

At UFC 170 Gibson lost to Aljamain Sterling by decision.1

At UFC Fight Night: Swanson vs. Stephens Gibson defeated Johnny Bedford by TKO.2

At UFC 178 Gibson lost to Manvel Gamburyan by submission.3
