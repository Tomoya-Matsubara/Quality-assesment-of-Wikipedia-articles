Joseph Nguy<U+00EA>n Tich Duc (February 22, 1938 – May 23, 2011) was the Roman Catholic bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Ban Me Thuot, Vietnam.

Ordained to the priesthood in 1967, Nguy<U+00EA>n Tich Duc became bishop in 1997 resigning in 2007.1
