The 20th Chess Olympiad, organized by the FIDE and comprising an open1 and women's tournament, as well as several events designed to promote the game of chess, took place between September 18 and October 13, 1972, in Skopje, Yugoslavia (present-day Macedonia). This Olympiad was the first major success for the Benko Gambit. In sixteen games, Black players scored ten wins and six draws with the opening.
