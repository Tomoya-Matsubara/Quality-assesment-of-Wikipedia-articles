Kiekrz is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rokietnica, within Pozna<U+0144> County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.1 It lies approximately south-east of Rokietnica and north-west of the regional capital Pozna<U+0144>.
