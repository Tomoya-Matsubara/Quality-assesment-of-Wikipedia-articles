For the Yorkshire cricketer, see John Waring

John "Jack" Waring is a former professional rugby league footballer of the 1940s who at representative level has played for England, and at club level for St Helens RLFC.

International Honours

Jack Waring won a cap for England while at St. Helens in 1940 against Wales.1
