Qais Abd al-Karim, also known as Abu Layla or Qays Samarra<U+2019>i, is a leading Palestinian activist.

He is a leader of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine, and a member of the Palestinian Legislative Council.

In 2006 he was one of two deputies elected from The Alternative list, an alliance between the DFLP and some small leftist parties.
