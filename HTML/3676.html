Arab Dizaji (, also Romanized as <U+2018>Arab D<U+012B>zaj<U+012B>; also known as <U+2018>Arab D<U+012B>zaj, Ar<U+0101>b Dizeh, <U+2018>Arab Dizehs<U+012B>, <U+2018>Arab-e D<U+012B>zaj, and <U+2018>Arab-e D<U+012B>zehs<U+012B>)1 is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 750, in 140 families.2
