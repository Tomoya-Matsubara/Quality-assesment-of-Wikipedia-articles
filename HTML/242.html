Haley Anderson (born November 20, 1991) is an American swimmer. At the 2009 US National Championships and World Championship Trials, Anderson placed second in the 800m freestyle,1 earning a place to compete at the 2009 World Aquatics Championships in Rome.2 Anderson's older sister Alyssa will also compete at the World Championships. Anderson has committed to swim at USC.
