Carl Wagener (23 December 1901 – 3 June 1988) was a highly decorated Generalmajor in the Wehrmacht during World War II. He was also a recipient of the Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross. The Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross was awarded to recognise extreme battlefield bravery or successful military leadership.

Awards and decorations

  • Iron Cross (1939)
    • 2nd Class
    • 1st Class
  • Eastern Front Medal
  • German Cross in Gold (20 February 1943)
  • Knight's Cross of the Iron Cross
    • Knight's Cross on 14 May 1944 as Oberst i.G. and Chef der Generalstab 1.Panzerarmee 1
