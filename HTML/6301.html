Baba Khanjar (, also Romanized as B<U+0101>b<U+0101> Khanjar)1 is a village in Shirin Su Rural District, Shirin Su District, Kabudarahang County, Hamadan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,669, in 336 families.2
