Osteomyelitis of the jaws is osteomyelitis (inflammation of the bone marrow) which occurs in the bones of the jaws (i.e. maxilla or the mandible). Historically, osteomyelitis of the jaws was a common complication of infections of the teeth. Before the antibiotic era, it was frequently a fatal condition.1
