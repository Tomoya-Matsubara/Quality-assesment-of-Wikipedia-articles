G<U+00E9>rard Paul Alain Feldzer (born 10 February 1944) is a consultant and aeronautical popularizer. He is a former French airline pilot, President of Comit<U+00E9> r<U+00E9>gional de tourisme d'<U+00CE>le-de-France (Regional Committee of <U+00CE>le-de-France Tourism) since May 2010,1 and Vice-Pr<U+00E9>sident of Ports de Paris (Ports of Paris).

He has also been Regional Advisor of <U+00CE>le-de-France for Europe <U+00C9>cologie2 since March 2010 and a close friend of Nicolas Hulot.

Biography

He started his career as an airline pilot at Air Alg<U+00E9>rie (1973<U+2013>1974) and moved to A<U+00E9>ropostale where he did night flights from 1974-1976. At Air France, he was a pilot on Sud Aviation Caravelle, Boeing 707 and 747 aircrafts, Captain (1989<U+2013>1992) then Captain Instructor (1992<U+2013>2004) on Airbus A310, A340 and A330.

In 1985, he applied for an Astronaut job at CNES and was one of ten finalists.

He became President of the A<U+00E9>ro-Club de France3 between 1995 and 2005. The centenary of the club took place during his presidency, and he organized a major exhibition of planes in Paris on the Champs-<U+00C9>lys<U+00E9>es to celebrate it.

Between 2005 and August 2010, G<U+00E9>rard Feldzer was head of the Mus<U+00E9>e de l'Air (French Air and Space Museum) at Le Bourget,.4 He made many animations and creations there including "Planete Pilote" ("Planet pilot"), the first space exhibition in the world aimed at 6-12 year olds.

He co-founded Zebunet (a micro-credit organisation for poor farmers in southern countries 5) in 2001 and he has been its Vice-President since then. He also founded the "Les ailes de la ville" scheme, which provides training and integration for young suburban people through repairing and constructing aircraft.6 As Founder and President of the "Transports passion" association, he has organized their annual meetings, exhibitions and events on ecomobility since 2005.7

On 1 January 2012, he became Officiers of the L<U+00E9>gion d'honneur8{{,}}.9

Media

Radio

Feldzer has had a weekly radio show on France Info since 2009. Recent episodes include Circulez! il y a le monde <U+00E0> voir (Move! there is the world to see)).10

TV series

Feldzer wrote and directed the following series:

  • Le Bar de l'escadrille (France 2, 1985)
  • Un ticket pour l'espace (France 2, 1990)
  • Les allum<U+00E9>s du sport (Canal+ 1992)

and co-wrote the transport series Transportez moi in 2011, which aired on LCP TV in 2012.
