Mario Masla<U+0107> (Serbian Cyrillic: Map<U+0438>o Mac<U+043B>a<U+045B>, born 9 September 1990 in Novi Sad) is a Serbian football defender playing with FK Borac <U+010C>a<U+010D>ak in the Serbian SuperLiga.

He made his senior debut in the season 2008-09 with FK Veternik before moving in summer 2009 to FK Borac <U+010C>a<U+010D>ak.1 He plays as central defender.

He is member of the Serbia national under-21 football team.2
