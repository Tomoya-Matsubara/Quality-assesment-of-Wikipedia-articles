Roman Rafailevich Khalilullov (; born February 2, 1993) is a Russian football striker who plays for FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.

He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Akademiya Tolyatti on April 24, 2011 in a game against FC Oktan Perm.1
