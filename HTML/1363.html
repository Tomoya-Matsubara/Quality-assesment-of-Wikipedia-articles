Definitely Maybe (Russian: <U+0417><U+0430> <U+043C><U+0438><U+043B><U+043B><U+0438><U+0430><U+0440><U+0434> <U+043B><U+0435><U+0442> <U+0434><U+043E> <U+043A><U+043E><U+043D><U+0446><U+0430> <U+0441><U+0432><U+0435><U+0442><U+0430>, Za milliard let do konza sveta, literal translation: One billion years before the end of the world) is a sci fi novel written in 1976 by Arkady and Boris Strugatsky. Noel Gallagher saw the promo ad in a pub and decided to name his band's, called Oasis, first album after the novel.

Plot summary

Dmitri Malyanov is a young astronomer, working on stellar astronomy. As he tries to resolve his equations, he feels that he is on the edge of a revolutionnary discovery.

But as he approaches the solution, strange phenomena start occurring all around him, preventing him from finishing his work: telephone ringing all the time, a beautiful girl claiming to be his wife's friend appearing on his doorstep and so on. Coincidence? Malyanov's friends, also researchers experimenting similar disturbances, don't think so...
