Ivan Tisci (born 22 March 1974) is an Italian footballer.

Tisci had spent 9 seasons at Serie B and briefly at Serie A.

After the relegation of Crotone from Serie B, he joined Foggia of Serie C1 in 1-year contract.1

In August 2009, he was signed by Avellino which recently relegated to Serie D.2
