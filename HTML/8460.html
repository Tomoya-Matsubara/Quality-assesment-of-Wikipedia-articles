Gol Bazu (, also Romanized as Gol B<U+0101>z<U+016B>; also known as Gol B<U+0101>zo)1 is a village in Nowsher-e Koshk-e Bijar Rural District, Khoshk-e Bijar District, Rasht County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 109, in 32 families.2
