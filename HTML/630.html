Kamaraj (, also Romanized as Kam<U+0101>raj and Kam<U+0101>rej; also known as Kamarady, Kam<U+0101>raj-e Markaz<U+012B>, Kam<U+0101>r<U+012B>j, and Komaradj)1 is a village in Kamaraj Rural District, Kamaraj and Konartakhteh District, Kazerun County, Fars Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,389, in 292 families.2
