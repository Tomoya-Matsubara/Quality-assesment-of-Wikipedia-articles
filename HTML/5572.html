The Elephant Princess is an Australian children's television series that first screened on Network Ten in 2008. The series was produced by Jonathan M. Shiff Productions. A second series is in production. 1

Plot

Alexandra Wilson is a suburban girl who suddenly discovers she is a princess of a mystical kingdom called Manjipur. Her exotic visitor Kuru and a magical elephant Anala help her master her new magical powers. Amanda, JB and Marcus are her bandmates and friends. Her family consists of parents Anita and Jim and her younger sister Zoe. 2

Episodes

Series one aired from November 2008 to April 2009, consisting of 26 episodes.
Series two is in production and will premiere in 2010. It will consist of 26 episodes, making the complete series 52 episodes.
