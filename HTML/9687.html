Kenneth Frank McNeill Uttley was born on (21 August, 1913 in Oamaru, Otago and died on 15 June, 1973) in Palmerston North, Manawatu. He was a New Zealand cricketer who played for Otago, Canterbury and Wellington in his 37 first-class matches. His son Ian Uttley played for the All Blacks in 1963.
