Canada-Thailand relations are foreign relations between Canada and Thailand. Both countries established diplomatic relations in 1947. Canada has an embassy in Bangkok and a consulate in Chiang Mai. Thailand has an embassy in Ottawa and 2 general consulates in Toronto and Vancouver.

Both countries are full members of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation.
