Lt. Col. Sir George Francis Osborne, 16th Baronet MC (27 July 1894 – 21 July 1960), was an English baronet and military.

Biography

He was the eldest son of Sir Francis Osborne, 15th Baronet and wife Kathleen Eliza Whitfield, and succeeded in the baronetcy on 23 October 1948.

He was educated at Repton School, Repton, Derbyshire, and at the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst, Sandhurst, Berkshire. He then fought in the World War I, where he was a war hero,1 was mentioned in despatches, was wounded twice, and was decorated with the award of Military Cross and gained the rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Royal Sussex Regiment.
