Paul Geary (born 24 July, 1963, in Medford, Massachusetts) was once the drummer for the hard rock band, Extreme. He was replaced in the group in 1994, by Mike Mangini.

Paul Geary later worked as a manager for other musicians.

Notable Bands he has managed or is currently mananging:

  • Godsmack
  • Cold
  • Smashing Pumpkins
