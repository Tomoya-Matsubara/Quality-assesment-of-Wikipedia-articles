Alexandra Koefoed (born 1978) is a Norwegian sailor. She was born in Oslo, and has represented the Royal Norwegian Yacht Club. She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, where she placed ninth in the Yngling class, together with Siren Sundby and Lise Birgitte Fredriksen.1
