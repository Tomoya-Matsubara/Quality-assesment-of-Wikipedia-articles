G<U+00E9>nies en herbe ('Budding Geniuses') was a Radio-Canada television program in which students representing their <U+00E9>cole secondaire (Quebec high schools) participated in trivia tournaments. Reach for the Top is an English Canadian equivalent.

After the end of the TV series the tournaments continued under the aegis of several regional school organisations.
