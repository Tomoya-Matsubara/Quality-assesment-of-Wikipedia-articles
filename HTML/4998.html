Uintah Basin Applied Technology College (UBATC) is a public technical training institution located in Roosevelt, Utah with an additional campus in Vernal and serving high school students and adults in Daggett, Duchesne, Uintah counties. UBATC is a part of the Utah College of Applied Technology and offers certificate programs in Business, Medical, Energy, Tranportation, and Trades and Industry.
