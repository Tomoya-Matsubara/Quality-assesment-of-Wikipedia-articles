Theodore 'Ted' Jay Tomblin1 (born February 2, 1971 in Logan, West Virginia) is an American politician and a Democratic member of the West Virginia House of Delegates representing District 24 since January 12, 2013.

Education

Tomblin attended Marshall University.

Elections

  • 2012 Redistricted to District 24, Tomblin ran in the May 8, 2012 Democratic Primary and placed second with 2,794 votes (25.1%),2 and placed first in the November 6, 2012 General election with 7,591 votes (52.6%) ahead of incumbent Democratic Representative Rupert Phillips.3
  • 2008 Initially in District 19, Tomblin ran in the twelve-way May 13, 2008 Democratic Primary but placed fifth;4 the top four went on to the November 4, 2008 General election.5
  • 2010 Tomblin ran in the eleven-way May 11, 2010 Democratic Primary but placed sixth;6 the top four went on to the six-way November 2, 2010 General election.7
