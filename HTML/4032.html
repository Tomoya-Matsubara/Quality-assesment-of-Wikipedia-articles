Gowhar Kola (, also Romanized as Gowhar Kol<U+0101>; also known as Gar Kol<U+0101>)1 is a village in Birun Bashm Rural District, Kelardasht District, Chalus County, Mazandaran Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 138, in 42 families.2
