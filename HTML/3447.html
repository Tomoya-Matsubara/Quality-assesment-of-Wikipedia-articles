Artur Eduardovich Nigmatullin (; born 17 May 1991) is a Russian football goalkeeper, who currently plays for FC Mordovia Saransk on loan from PFC CSKA Moscow.

He made his professional debut for FC Mordovia Saransk on April 4, 2011 in a Russian First Division game against FC SKA-Energiya Khabarovsk.1
