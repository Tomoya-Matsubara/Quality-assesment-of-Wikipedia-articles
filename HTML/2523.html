Siah Karan (, also Romanized as S<U+012B><U+0101>h Kar<U+0101>n)1 is a village in Baraghan Rural District, Chendar District, Savojbolagh County, Alborz Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 32, in 10 families.2
