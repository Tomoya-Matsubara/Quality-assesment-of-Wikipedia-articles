Lietuvos krep<U+0161>inio lyga 2014<U+2013>15 (LKL) is the 22nd season of the top basketball league of Lithuania.

Competition Format

Regular season

During the regular season, all teams will play 40 games.

Playoff round

The top eight teams in the regular season round will join the playoffs in the quarterfinals, that are played in a best-of-three games format. The semifinals are also played in this format.

The final round is played between the two winners of the semifinals. The final series for the first place are played in a best-of-seven format, while the series for the third place are played in a best-of-five format.1
