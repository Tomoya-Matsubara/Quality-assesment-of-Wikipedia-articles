Pirojpur Sadar () is an Upazila of Pirojpur District in the Division of Barisal, Bangladesh.

Geography

Pirojpur Sadar is located at . It has 41893 units of house hold and total area 278.37 km<U+00B2>.

Demographics

As of 1991 Bangladesh census, Pirojpur Sadar has a population of 225156. Males constitute are 50.93% of the population, and females 49.07%. This Upazila's eighteen up population is 116628. Pirojpur Sadar has an average literacy rate of 50.7% (7+ years), and the national average of 32.4% literate. 1

Administrative

Pirojpur Sadar has 13 Unions/Wards, 130 Mauzas/Mahallas, and 143 vilages.
