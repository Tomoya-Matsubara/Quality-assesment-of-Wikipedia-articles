is a city located in Yamagata Prefecture, Japan.

As of 2008, the city has an estimated population of 27,380 and a population density of 139 persons per km<U+00B2>. The total area is 196.83 km<U+00B2>.

The city was founded on November 1, 1954.

The city is twinned with Yakutsk, Russia.1
