Bid Chah (, also Romanized as B<U+012B>d Ch<U+0101>h; also known as B<U+012B>r Ch<U+0101>h)1 is a village in Chupanan Rural District, Anarak District, Nain County, Isfahan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported.2
