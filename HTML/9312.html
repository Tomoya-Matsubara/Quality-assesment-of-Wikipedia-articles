Ebenezer Knowlton Fogg (October 24, 1837 <U+2013> April 21, 1900) was an American shoe retailer and politician, who served as a member of the Massachusetts House of Representatives, and as a member of the Common Council, Board of Aldermen and as the 24th Mayor of Lynn, Massachusetts.

Early life

Fogg was born on October 24, 1837 in Norwood, Massachusetts to Jeremiah Fogg and Abigail (Hill) Fogg.1

Postmaster of Lynn

Fogg was the Postmaster of Lynn, Massachusetts from June 1, 1898 until his death on April 21, 1900.2
