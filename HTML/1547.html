The 2013<U+2013>14 Zenit season is the 18th successive season that the club will play in the Russian Premier League, the highest tier of association football in Russia. They will participate in the Russian Premier League and Russian Cup aswell as entering the UEFA Champions League at the 3rd qualifying round. They started the season with a 3 – 0 defeat to CSKA Moscow on 12 July in the 2013 Super Cup.
