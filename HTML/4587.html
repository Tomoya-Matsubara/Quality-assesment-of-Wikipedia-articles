Patricia Irene (Irina) Dunn is an Australian writer, and served in the Australian Senate between 1988 and 1990. In politics she was Senator for New South Wales first representing the Nuclear Disarmament Party and then as an independent. Has coined the famous phrase: "A woman without a man is like a fish without a bicycle".
