Melanie Bruce (born 22 January 1972 in London) was an English ice dancer. With partner Andrew Place, she won the gold medal at the 1990 British Figure Skating Championships. They finished 17th at the 1992 European Figure Skating Championships, 17th at the 1992 Winter Olympics, and 19th at the 1992 World Figure Skating Championships.
