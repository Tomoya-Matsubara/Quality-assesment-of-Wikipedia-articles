Jacqueline Comerre, n<U+00E9>e Paton (May 1, 1859 <U+2013> 1955) was a French painter and sculptor, and the wife of the painter Leon Comerre.

Comerre-Paton was born in Paris and studied at the <U+00C9>cole des Beaux-arts under Alexandre Cabanel.

Her painting Mistletoe was included in the 1905 book Women Painters of the World.1

File:Jacqueline Comerre-Paton - Mistletoe.jpg|Mistletoe

Comerre-Paton died in Paris.
