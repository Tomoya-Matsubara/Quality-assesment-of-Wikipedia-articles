Mingo Fishtrap is a Soul and funk band based in Austin, Texas.1234567 They were formed in the mid-1990s in Denton, Texas. The band consists of Roger Blevins, Jr. Guitar/Lead Vocals, Chip Vayenas Drums/Vocals, Dane Farnsworth Organ/Keyboards, Mikel Urdy Percussion, Roger Blevins Sr. Bass, Steve Butts Trumpet/Fl<U+00FC>gelhorn, Dan Bechdolt Tenor Saxophone, Greg Wilson Baritone Saxophone/Alto Saxophone. They have, with a few personnel changes over the years, released six albums and one concert video: Succotash 1997, From The Private Bag 2000, EP2 2004, Yesterday 2005,8 a concert DVD; Live at the Granada 2006, EP3 2008 and In The Meantime 2010.
