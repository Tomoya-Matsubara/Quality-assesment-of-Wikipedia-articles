Qiangdui () is a village and township in Bainang County, in the Shigatse prefecture-level city of the Tibet Autonomous Region of China. At the time of the 2010 census, the township had a population of 2,369.1, it had 7 villages under its administration. The Nyang River flows to the south of the main village.
