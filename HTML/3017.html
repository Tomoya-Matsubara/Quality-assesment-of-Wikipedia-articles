Utirik Airport is a public use airstrip on Utirik Island in Utirik Atoll, Marshall Islands. This airstrip is assigned the location identifier 03N by the FAA1 and UTK by the IATA.2

Facilities

Utirik Airport is at an elevation of 4 feet (1.2 m) above mean sea level. The runway is designated 07/25 with a coral gravel surface measuring 2,400 by 50 feet (732 x 15 m).3 There are no aircraft based at Utirik.4
