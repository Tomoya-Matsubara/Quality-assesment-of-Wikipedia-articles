Stuart Smith is a New Zealand politician who was elected to the New Zealand parliament at the 2014 general election as a representative of the New Zealand National Party.

Political career

Smith is a former chairman of the New Zealand Winegrowers Association. In 2013 he contested the National Party selection for the Kaik<U+014D>ura seat, defeating incumbent Colin King.1 He was elected at the 2014 election with a preliminary majority of 11,510 votes.2
