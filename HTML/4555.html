Adam Tomasiak (born February 15, 1953) is a Polish rower who competed in the 1976 Summer Olympics and in the 1980 Summer Olympics.

He was born in Biesal, Gmina Gietrzwa<U+0142>d.

In 1976 he was a crew member of the Polish boat which finished eighth in the coxed fours event.

Four years later he won the bronze medal with the Polish boat in the 1980 coxed fours competition. In the same Olympics he also competed with the Polish team in the 1980 eights contest and finished ninth.
