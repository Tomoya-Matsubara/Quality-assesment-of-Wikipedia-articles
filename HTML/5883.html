Melichrus is a genus of flowering plants in the family Ericaceae. The genus is endemic to eastern Australia.1

Species include:2

  • Melichrus adpressus A.Cunn. ex DC.
  • Melichrus erubescens A.Cunn. ex DC.
  • Melichrus hirsutus J.B.Williams
  • Melichrus procumbens (Cav.) Druce
  • Melichrus sp. Gibberagee
  • Melichrus sp. Newfoundland State Forest
  • Melichrus urceolatus R.Br.
