Paigham is a 1959 Hindi movie produced and directed by S S Vassan. The film stars Dilip Kumar, Vyjayanthimala, Raaj Kumar, Motilal and Johnny Walker. The films music is by C. Ramachandra. This was the first time Dilip and Raaj Kumar appeared in a film together. They later appeared together once again over three decades later for the 1991 film Saudagar.

Awards

Filmfare Best Dialogue Award for Ramanand Sagar
