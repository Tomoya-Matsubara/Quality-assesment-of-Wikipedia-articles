Butcher Babies EP is the first EP released by the American heavy metal band Butcher Babies. It was self-released on May 29, 2012. The EP was produced by Andrew Murdock.1

Reception

Rock Journalist Keith Valcourt stated on behalf of the Butcher Babies: <U+201C>The Hottest Band in the World<U+201D> in his review of a show. The Butcher Babies deliver a loud crashing blend of heavy metal, punk and thrash that recalls Pantera, adding their stage show embodies the horror antics of Alice Cooper and Rob Zombie. Carla and Heidi don<U+2019>t merely sing: they assault the crowd with a blinding flash of aggression and abuse. And the crowd loves them for it.<U+201D>2

Release and promotion

On May 1, 2012, the Butcher Babies Premiered a New Song, <U+201C>Mr. Slowdeath<U+201D>.3 On May 24, they released their first music video for "Mr. Slowdeath", produced by Mudrock (Avenged Sevenfold, Godsmack).4
