Garrison Independent School District is a public school district based in Garrison, Texas (USA).

The district is located in northeastern Nacogdoches County and extends into a southeastern Rusk County.

Garrison ISD has three campuses - Garrison High (Grades 9-12), Garrison Middle (Grades 6-8), and Garrison Elementary (Grades PK-5).
