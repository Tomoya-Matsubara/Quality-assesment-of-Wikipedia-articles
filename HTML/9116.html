Li Meng (<U+674E><U+8499>) was an officer who served under Dong Zhuo during the Three Kingdoms period of China. After Dong Zhuo's death, he opened Chang'an's gate together with Wang Fang, allowing Dong Zhuo's loyalist army into the city. Later, he fought with Ma Chao, but was caught and beheaded.
