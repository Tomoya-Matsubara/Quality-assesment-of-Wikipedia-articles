Lee Sze Ming (; born February 5 1979 in Hong Kong) is a Hong Kong football player who currently plays for Eastern (loan from Convoy Sun Hei). He can play in varies of positions (all attacking positisions), mostly as a forward and right wing. He scored for Hong Kong against Brazil in the 2005 Carlsberg Cup. 1

Lee also plays for the Hong Kong 5-on-side team. Fans in Hong Kong often call him the King of 5-on-side.
