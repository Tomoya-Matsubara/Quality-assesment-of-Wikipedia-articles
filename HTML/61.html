Paul-Louis Roubert (born 1967) is an associate researcher at the Laboratoire d'histoire visuelle contemporaine, senior lecturer in photography at Paris 8 University and, since December 2010, president of the Soci<U+00E9>t<U+00E9> fran<U+00E7>aise de photographie.12

Roubert was awarded a doctorate in 2004 on the basis of his thesis on L'Introduction du mod<U+00E8>le photographique dans la critique d'art en France which led to L'Image sans qualit<U+00E9>s published in 2006. He has been closely connected to the Soci<U+00E9>t<U+00E9> fran<U+00E7>aise de photographie whose bulletin he edited from 1999 to 2004. Since 1996, He has been a founding member of the journal Etudes photographiques. He has lectured in the history of photography at Paris 8, Paris 1 and at the <U+00C9>cole des hautes <U+00E9>tudes en sciences sociales. In December 2010, he was unanimously elected president of the Soci<U+00E9>t<U+00E9> fran<U+00E7>aise de photographie.34
