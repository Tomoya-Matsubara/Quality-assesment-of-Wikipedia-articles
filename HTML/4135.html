7408 Yoshihide (provisional designation: 1989 SB) is a main-belt minor planet. It was discovered by Yoshikane Mizuno and Toshimasa Furuta in Kani, Gifu Prefecture, Japan, on September 23, 1989. It is named after Yoshihide Hayashi, a Japanese amateur astronomer.
