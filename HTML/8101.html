Lithilaria is a genus of moths of the Noctuidae family.

Selected Species

  • Lithilaria anomozancla (Turner, 1944)
  • Lithilaria melanostrotum (Turner, 1906)
  • Lithilaria ossicolor Rosenstock, 1885
  • Lithilaria proestans (T.P. Lucas, 1895)
