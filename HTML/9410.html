The Women's 67 kg is a competition featured at the 2011 World Taekwondo Olympic Qualification Tournament, and was held at the Sarhadchi Olympic Center in Baku, Azerbaijan on June 30. The first three ranked athletes qualify their NOCs a place each at the 2012 Olympic Games.
