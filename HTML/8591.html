The Academy for Math, Engineering, and Science (AMES) is a charter school located within Cottonwood High School, in Salt Lake City, UT, is part of a state initiative known as the New Century High Schools. The school opened in 2003, and receives support from not only the state of Utah, but also from the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, and various other foundations. The school has placed importance upon offering students who desire a better education an early college experience, and has provided students with college classes. It possesses no inter-mural sports teams, and has an annual enrollment of 100-250 students.
