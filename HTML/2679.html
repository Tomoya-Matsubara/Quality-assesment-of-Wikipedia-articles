The 2011 FINA Swimming World Cup is a series of seven (7), two-day, short course meets in seven different cities in October and November 2011. Arena is again the title sponsor for the series, with Omega serving as official timer.

Meets

As of August 2011, the 2011 World Cup consists of the following seven meets:1
