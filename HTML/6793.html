Alvin Hph-wayuo is a fictional character in David Brin's Uplift Universe. He is a Hoon, the son of Mu-phauwq and Yowg-wayuo, from the village of Wuphon's Port in fictional planet Jijo. He is a humicker <U+2014> he is fond of human culture, language and literature, especially science fiction, so he adopted the nickname Alvin taken from the main protagonist of <U+201C>The City and the Stars<U+201D> by Arthur C. Clarke. He is one of the major point of view characters in the Uplift Storm Trilogy and the only one whose story is narrated from the first person.
