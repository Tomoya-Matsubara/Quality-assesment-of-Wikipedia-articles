Danuta Pietraszewska (born May 08, 1947 in Ruda <U+015A>l<U+0105>ska) is a Polish politician. She was elected to Sejm on September 25, 2005 getting 9841 votes in 31 Katowice district, candidating from Platforma Obywatelska list.
