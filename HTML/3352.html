Roberto Tozzi (born 17 December,1958) was an Italian athlete who competed mainly in the 400 metres.

He competed for an Italy in the 1980 Summer Olympics held in Moscow, Soviet Union in the 4 x 400 metre relay where he won the bronze medal with his team mates Stefano Malinverni, Mauro Zuliani and Pietro Mennea.
