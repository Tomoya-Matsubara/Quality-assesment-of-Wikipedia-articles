Lubo<U+0161> Posp<U+00ED><U+0161>il (born 6 September 1950 in Jesen<U+00ED>k) is a Czech rock singer-songwriter. In 1970, he was founding member of C&K Vocal and since he left this band, he released many solo albums. In early eighties, he was also short-time member of Blue Effect. His most recent album Chutn<U+00E1><U+0161> po ciz<U+00ED>m ovoci was released in 2011.1 He will release a new album in September 2014.2
