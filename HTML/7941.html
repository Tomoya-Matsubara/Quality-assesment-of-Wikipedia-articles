This is a list of media related to the Halo series. Central to the Halo series are the three first-person shooter video games Halo: Combat Evolved, Halo 2 and Halo 3, with novels, soundtracks, and other media also available. The story of the series is about the Master Chief, a cybernetically enhanced human super-soldier and his artificial intelligence (AI) companion, Cortana, as the humans of a futuristic universe battle the Covenant, a theocratic alliance of alien races. The Halo series has been used as the basis for machinima production such as Red vs. Blue and fan fiction, however this list covers only media produced or endorsed by the creator, Bungie Studios.

The Halo video games have been highly successful and influential; the first game was labeled the killer application of Microsoft's Xbox and was credited with selling many consoles.12 Halo: Combat Evolved is also noted for its intuitive control scheme that has been used by many console first person shooters since.3 Halo 2 sold 2.4 million copies making US$125 million in the first 24 hours after its release beating the record for highest grossing entertainment release,4 release however Halo 3 grossed US$170 million on its first day, $45 million more.5 Halo 2 and Halo 3 are also the best selling titles of their respective consoles, the Xbox and the Xbox 360.67
