Tazeh Kand (, also Romanized as T<U+0101>zeh Kand)1 is a village in Chaharduli Rural District, Keshavarz District, Shahin Dezh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 25, in 5 families.2
