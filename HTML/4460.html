'''''To levende og en d<U+00F8>d ''''' is a 1937 Norweigan thriller film directed by Gyda Christensen and Tancred Ibsen and starring Hans Jacob Nilsen, Unni Torkildsen and Jan Vaage. It is based on the 1931 novel To levende og en d<U+00F8>d by Sigurd Christiansen. A post office worker is left wrestling with his conscience following a robbery at his workplace.
