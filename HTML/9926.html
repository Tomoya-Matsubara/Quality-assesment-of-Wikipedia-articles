Janet Carafa is a mime artist based in New York City who also runs New York Entertainment Connection, an event entertainment and production company. She has studied with Marcel Marceau and was a lead performer with the American Mime Theatre for eight years. She has also directed the Movement and Mime Theatre.

Originally from Connecticut and a graduate of the University of Colorado, Ms. Carafa has performed with the New York City Mime Company and the Valley Studio for Mime in Wisconsin.
