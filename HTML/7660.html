Irache Quintanal (born 18 September 1978 in Tarragona) is a Spanish shot putter.

She finished fifteenth at the 2001 Summer Universiade and eighth at the 2002 IAAF World Cup. She also competed at the 2004 Olympic Games and the 2007 World Championships without reaching the finals.

Her personal best throw is 18.20 metres, achieved in July 2007 in Barcelona. This is the current Spanish record.
