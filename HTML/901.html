Anastasiya Sienina (born 1991 in Simferopol, Crimea) is a Ukrainian model and current Miss Crimea 2010. Sienina placed second runner-up at the Miss Ukraine 2010 pageant.1 Later she was appointed the Miss Crimea title and also became the first delegate to represent Crimea in an international beauty pageant as she attended Miss Earth 2010.23

Sienina made the Top 5 in the Miss Earth talent competition.4
