Doln<U+00E1> Poruba is a village and municipality in Tren<U+010D><U+00ED>n District in the Tren<U+010D><U+00ED>n Region of north-western Slovakia.

History

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1355.

Geography

The municipality lies at an altitude of 427 metres and covers an area of 22.688 km<U+00B2>. It has a population of about 845 people.
