Nausodis eldership (Lithuanian: Nausod<U+017E>io seni<U+016B>nija) is an eldership in Plung<U+0117> district municipality to the southwest from Plung<U+0117>. The administrative center is Varkaliai.

As of 2003, there were 3678 inhabitants live in this eldership.
