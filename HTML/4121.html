Charqueadas is a municipality in the state of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. It is located on the right side of the Jacu<U+00ED> River in the southern-central part of the state, in the region Carbon<U+00ED>fera, about 60 km from Porto Alegre and beblonging to the metropolitan area of the state capital. The area of the municipality is 214.80 km2 and a population of 33,808 (2006 estimate, IBGE), making it the largest municipality in area and population in the region. It borders to the south Arroio dos Ratos, to the north Triunfo, to the east Eldorado do Sul and to the west S<U+00E3>o Jer<U+00F4>nimo.
