Stan Mayfield (November 12, 1955 <U+2013> September 30, 2008) in Pensacola, Florida.

Mayfield was a Representative in the House of Representatives of the U.S. state of Florida. He received his Bachelors degree in Civil Engineering from the University of Florida in 1987. It should also be noted he lived in Vero Beach, Florida with his family. Representative Mayfield died September 30, 2008, at the age of 52, after a long battle with cancer.
