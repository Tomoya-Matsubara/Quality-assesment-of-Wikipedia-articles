Kumla Church (Swedish: Kumla kyrka) is a limestone church, built in the 12th century. It's located at the lake T<U+00E5>kern in Kumla socken in Mj<U+00F6>lby Municipality in <U+00D6>sterg<U+00F6>tland, Sweden.
