Val-d'Auzon is a commune in France, in the Aube d<U+00E9>partement. It is approximately 30 kilometers northeast of Troyes. It was created on May 1, 1972 from the amalgamation of the communes of Auzon-les-Marais, Montangon and Villehardouin.
