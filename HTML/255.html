Mounkeo Oraboun is a Laotian politician. As of 2010, he is the current Minister of Information and Culture in Laos. He attends many important events in the country and southeast Asia and is an active member of ASEAN, chairing the 10th conference in Vientiane in November 2009.1As the Minister of Culture, Oraboun busily attends a diversity of events and inaugurations across Laos, ranging from attending newspaper anniversaries, to hotel openings.23
