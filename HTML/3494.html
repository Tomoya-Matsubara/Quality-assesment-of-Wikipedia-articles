Bridge to Nowhere is a 1986 movie directed and co-written by Ian Mune, and produced by Larry Parr.

It was filmed on New Zealand's North Island.1

The cast includes:

  • Philip Gordon
  • Matthew Hunter
  • Stephen Judd
  • Bruno Lawrence
  • Shelly Luxford
  • Alison Routledge
  • Margaret Umbers
  • Norman Fairley
  • Faye Flegg
  • Shirley Gruar
  • Peter McCauley
