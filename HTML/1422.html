Filip was an eleventh century bishop of P<U+0142>ock, Poland.1

According to Jan D<U+0142>ugosz he was from the Doliwczyk<U+00F3>w Polish Noble family and was appointed by Pope Paschal II. He is known to history through some papal correspondence,2 but exact dates for his bishopric are uncertain. He was probably ordained 1099 or 1100 and remained Bishop unto 1007AD or 1112AD.
