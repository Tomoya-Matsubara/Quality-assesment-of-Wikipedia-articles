deafplanet is a Canadian television series for children in American Sign Language (ASL). It was created by marblemedia in collaboration with TVOntario and the Canadian Cultural Society of the Deaf. A French-language version of the show is also produced, using Quebec Sign Language (LSQ).

The TV series aired in Canada on provincial broadcasters TVOntario, Access, SCN and Knowledge.
