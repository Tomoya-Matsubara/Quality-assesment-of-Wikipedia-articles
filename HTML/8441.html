Valpara<U+00ED>so Province is one of the provinces of the Valpara<U+00ED>so Region, Chile.

Communes

The province consists of 7 communes (Spanish: comunas):

  • Valpara<U+00ED>so
  • Vi<U+00F1>a del Mar
  • Conc<U+00F3>n
  • Quintero
  • Puchuncav<U+00ED>
  • Casablanca
  • Juan Fern<U+00E1>ndez

Quilpu<U+00E9> and Villa Alemana were transferred to Marga Marga Province in 2010.
