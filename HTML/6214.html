The Bishop of Melanesia is the head of the Anglican Church of the Province of Melanesia.

List of holders

  1. John Coleridge Patteson, 1861-1871
  2. John Richardson Selwyn, 1877-1892
  3. Cecil Wilson, 1894-1911
  4. Cecil Wood, 1911-1919
  5. John Manwaring Steward, 1919-1928
  6. Frederick M. Molyneux, 1928-1932
  7. Walter Hubert Baddeley, 1932-1947
  8. S. G. Caulton, 1947-1958
  9. Alfred Thomas Hill, 1958-
