Col<U+00E9>gio Santa Maria is a Catholic secondary school founded in S<U+00E3>o Paulo, Brazil by the Sisters of the Holy Cross in 1947. It is considered one of the best Paulist schools by Veja magazine.

In the past, it was a girls-only school.
