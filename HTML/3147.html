Burleson High School is a four year public high school consisting of grades 9-12 located in Burleson, Texas and is part of the Burleson Independent School District.

History

In 1901, Burleson's first school, the Red Oak Academy was constructed. It was destroyed by fire in 1909. The State of Texas granted a charter for an independent school district and the citizens of Burleson voted to construct a new school. By 1910 the new school was opened. In 1997, a new building for the high school was built at 100 Elk Drive.

Achievements

Burleson was a state semifinalist in the 4A state title in football in 2001. In 2006, Burleson won the 4-A District Championship in football over Mansfield Summit High School. Burleson High School is also where the famous See You at the Pole tradition began and where the famous Kelly Clarkson graduated from.

Media

"Elk TV" first aired in the 2005-2006 school year. It is the school's weekly news program which is written, directed and edited by the students in the broadcast journalism class.
