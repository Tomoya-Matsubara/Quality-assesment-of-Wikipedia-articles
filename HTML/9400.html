Ben Reynolds (born January 7, 1982 in Lithgow, New South Wales) is an Australian rugby league player for the Wests Tigers in the National Rugby League competition, he has also previously played for the Penrith Panthers. His position of choice is at half-back.

Career highlights

  • Junior Club: Lithgow Shamrocks
  • First Grade Debut: Round 15, Penrith v Brisbane at Penrith Stadium, June 2, 2001
  • First Grade Record: 21 appearances scoring 6 tries & 13 goals
