John Haskell House, also known as The Hermitage, was a historic home located near New Windsor in Orange County, New York. It was built about 1724, and was a 2 1/2-story log dwelling with a rear ell. It had a gable roof and large interior chimney at each end.1

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1973.2 The building was demolished sometime after 2009.
