Jed Graef (born May 1, 1942) is an American swimmer and olympic champion. He competed at the 1964 Olympic Games in Tokyo, where he received a gold medal in 200 m backstroke.1

Awards

He was inducted into the International Swimming Hall of Fame in Fort Lauderdale, Florida in 1988.
