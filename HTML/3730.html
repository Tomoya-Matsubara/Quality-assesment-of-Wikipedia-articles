On Fire was the first single from Lloyd Banks' album, The Hunger for More. It featured 50 Cent's vocals on the chorus, although 50 Cent is not officially credited as being a guest appearance on it. The single peaked at number 8 on the Billboard Hot 100 and went gold. The song was produced by Eminem and Kwam<U+00E9>.
