Bauma is a railway station in the Swiss canton of Zurich and municipality of Bauma. The station is situated at the junction of the T<U+00F6>sstal railway line, which is still in full use, and the Uerikon to Bauma railway (UeBB), which is partly closed and partly used as a heritage railway.123

Bauma station is an intermediate station on the Zurich S-Bahn route S26, which operates between Rapperswil and Winterthur.4

Bauma station is also the terminus of services of the Dampfbahn-Verein Z<U+00FC>rcher Oberland (DVZO), who operate to Hinwil, over the former UeBB, with trains normally hauled by steam locomotives. The UeBB line beyond Hinwil, towards Uerikon, was closed in 1948 and little now remains.56
