Acta entomologica Serbica is a peer-reviewed1 scientific journal covering entomology published by the Entomological Society of Serbia2. It publishes mainly original research papers3.

The journal was first established as Glasnik Entomolo<U+0161>kog dru<U+0161>tva Kraljevine Srba, Hrvata i Slovenaca [Bulletin of the Entomological Society of the Kingdom of Serbs, Croats and Slovenes] in May 1926; then renamed to Glasnik Jugoslovenskog entomolo<U+0161>kog dru<U+0161>tva [Bulletin of the Yugoslav Entomological Society] in 1929; it was re-established as Acta entomologica Jugoslavica in 1971 and finally as Acta entomologica serbica in December 1996.4
