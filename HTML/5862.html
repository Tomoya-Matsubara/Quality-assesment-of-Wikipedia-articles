FM 1576 is a 24-mile farm to market road entirely in Hudspeth County in West Texas.

Route description

FM 1576 runs from northward before ending at Otero Co. Rd. G005 at the Texas / New Mexico state line.123

History

The FM 1576 designation was originally assigned in 1949 to a short road near Brownsville in Cameron County. This mileage was transferred to in 1956.

In 1957, part of and , to the east of Dell City, were canceled and combined to form FM 1576. In 1960, FM 1576 had the mileage from added to it, and it was extended southward approximately 10.1 miles to US 62 / US 180. In 1973, it was extended to the northeast to its current northern terminus.4
