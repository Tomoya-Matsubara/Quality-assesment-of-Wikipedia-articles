Sari Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as S<U+0101>r<U+012B> Qeshl<U+0101>q)1 is a village in Qeshlaq-e Gharbi Rural District, Aslan Duz District, Parsabad County, Ardabil Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 189, in 33 families.2
