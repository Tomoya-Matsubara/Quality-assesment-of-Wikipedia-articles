La Argentinita is the stage name of Encarnaci<U+00F3>n L<U+00F3>pez J<U+00FA>lvez (Buenos Aires, March 3, 1898 <U+2013> New York, September 24, 1945), a Spanish dancer and singer. Among her performances was as the Butterfly in the premiere of Federico Garc<U+00ED>a Lorca's El maleficio de la mariposa. She partnered with Frederico Rey who, as Freddy Wittop, later enjoyed a successful and award-winning career as a theatrical costume designer.

The Spanish choreographer and ballerina Pilar L<U+00F3>pez J<U+00FA>lvez was her sister. She chose the name La Argentinita in deference to dancer La Argentina.1
