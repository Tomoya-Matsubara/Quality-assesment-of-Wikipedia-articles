Mir Fazlollah (, also Romanized as M<U+012B>r Fa<U+1E95>loll<U+0101>h and M<U+012B>r Fazloll<U+0101>h; also known as M<U+012B>r Fey<U+1E95>oll<U+0101>h)1 is a village in Titkanlu Rural District, Khabushan District, Faruj County, North Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 109, in 28 families.2
