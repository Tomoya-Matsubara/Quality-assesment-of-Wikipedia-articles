Tappeh Saremi (, also Romanized as Tappeh <U+015E><U+0101>rem<U+012B>)1 is a village in Marhemetabad-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 158, in 32 families.2
