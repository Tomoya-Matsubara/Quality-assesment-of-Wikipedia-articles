Natalya Kalashinkova is a Soviet sprint canoer who competed in the late 1970s and early 1980s. She won three medals at the ICF Canoe Sprint World Championships with a gold (K-2 500 m: 1979) and two silvers (K-2 500 m:1978, K-4 500 m: 1983).
