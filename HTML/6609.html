Aaron Silverman is an American chef, owner of Rose's Luxury in Washington, DC.

Silverman has been the subject of a profile in the Washington Post, which characterized his work as mastery of 'the art of serious play',1 and Rose's Luxury has been reviewed as 'the best new restaurant in America' by Bon App<U+00E9>tit.2
