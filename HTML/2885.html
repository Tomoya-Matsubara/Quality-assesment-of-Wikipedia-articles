Cheshmeh-ye Papi (, also Romanized as Cheshmeh-ye P<U+0101>p<U+012B>; also known as Cheshmeh-ye P<U+0101><U+2019><U+012B>)1 is a village in Dehpir-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 263, in 49 families.2
