Bevier Memorial Building is a historic institutional building built originially for the Rochester Athaneaum and Mechanics Institute located at Rochester in Monroe County, New York. It is a three and a half brick story with ceramic trim designed by Claude Bragdon and completed in 1910.1

It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1973.2
