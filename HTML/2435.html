Darin Su (, also Romanized as Dar<U+012B>n S<U+016B>)1 is a village in Nazarkahrizi Rural District, Nazarkahrizi District, Hashtrud County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 245, in 62 families.2
