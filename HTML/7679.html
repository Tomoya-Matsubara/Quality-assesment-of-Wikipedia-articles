Mapp and Lucia is a British drama television series that is set to first broadcast on BBC One. The three-part series, adapted by Steve Pemberton and directed by Diarmuid Lawrence, is based on E. F. Benson's collection of novels of the same name.12

Cast

  • Anna Chancellor as Emmeline Lucas
  • Steve Pemberton as Georgie Pillson
  • Miranda Richardson as Elizabeth Mapp

Production

Mapp and Lucia was commissioned by Charlotte Moore and Ben Stephenson for BBC One.3 Filming will begin in Rye, East Sussex in summer 2014.
