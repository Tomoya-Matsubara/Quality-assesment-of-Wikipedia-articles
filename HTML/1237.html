The Baltimore Declaration was a piece written by six Episcopal priests in the Diocese of Maryland in 1991 regarding affirmation of orthodox Christianity. It is sometimes used as a manifesto by Episcopal factions which wish to reaffirm orthodox Christian principles within the Episcopal Church in the United States of America.

Signers

  • The Rev. Ronald S. Fisher
  • The Rev. Alvin F. Kimel, Jr.
  • The Rev. R. Gary Matthewes-Green
  • The Rev. William N. McKeachie
  • The Rev. Frederick J. Ramsay
  • The Rev. Philip Burwell Roulette
