The Sumers Lodge in Glenwood Springs, Colorado is a rustic log house built in 1935. The house was built for New York financier George Sumers. It was designed by Chilson Aldrich, and some of its furnishings were designed and made by Thomas C. Molesworth.12

The Sumers Lodge is a large two story rectangular log structure with a projecting sun porch on the entrance side.3
