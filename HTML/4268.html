Uthamapalayam taluk is a taluk of Theni district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. The headquarters of the taluk is the town of Uthamapalayam.

Population

According to the 2001 census, the taluk of Uthamapalayam had a population of 376,963 with 189,271 males and 187,692 females. There were 992 women for every 1000 men. The taluk had a literacy rate of 72.15. The total number of households was 94,744.
