The 65th Annual Tony Awards will take place on Sunday, June 12, 2011 and will celebrate the best of Broadway. They will be held at the Beacon Theatre, ending a fourteen-year tradition of having the ceremony at Radio City Music Hall.1 The Awards will be broadcast live on CBS at 8:00pm (eastern).23

Eligibility

Shows opening during the 2010 - 2011 Broadway season before April 28, 2011 are eligible.4
