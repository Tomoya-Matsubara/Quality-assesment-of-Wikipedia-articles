The Bellevue District (District 13) is a municipal district in the city of Gatineau, Quebec. It is represented on Gatineau City Council by Richard C<U+00F4>t<U+00E9>.

The district is located in the Gatineau sector of the city and includes the newer developments in the north-central part of the sector as well as rural area.

  2. Sylvie Goneau (2009-present)
