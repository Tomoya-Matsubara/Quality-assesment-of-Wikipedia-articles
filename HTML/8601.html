Sporminore (Ladin: Sporpiciol, ) is a comune (municipality) in Trentino in the northern Italian region Trentino-Alto Adige/S<U+00FC>dtirol, located about 20 km northwest of Trento. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 695 and an area of 17.5 km<U+00B2>.1

Sporminore borders the following municipalities: Ton, Campodenno and Spormaggiore.
