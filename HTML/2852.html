The family of Saposa-Tinputz languages is a subgroup of the Nehan-North Bougainville Solomonic languages. It consists of four languages. They are largely spoken on the island of Bougainville in Papua New Guinea.

Components

  • Hahon
  • Saposa
  • Teop
  • Tinputz
