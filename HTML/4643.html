Chenaran (, also Romanized as Chen<U+0101>r<U+0101>n; also known as Chin<U+0101>r<U+0101>n)1 is a village in Khezel-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Kangavar County, Kermanshah Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 77, in 18 families.2
