Colli del Tronto is a comune (municipality) in the Province of Ascoli Piceno in the Italian region Marche, located about 80 km south of Ancona and about 14 km northeast of Ascoli Piceno. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 3,290 and an area of 5.9 km<U+00B2>.1

Colli del Tronto borders the following municipalities: Ancarano, Ascoli Piceno, Castorano, Spinetoli.
