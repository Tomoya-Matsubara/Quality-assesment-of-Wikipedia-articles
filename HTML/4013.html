Killzone: Shadow Fall is an upcoming video game by Guerrilla Games for the Playstation 4 console. It will be the fourth game in the Killzone video game series.12

Plot

Killzone: Shadow Fall will be set around 30 years after the events of the previous Killzone games. The game features the Vektan and Helghast factions living in a large city that is divided by a security wall.3 The Helghast are fighting for their right to exist, while the Vektans are fighting for survival.4

Release

Killzone: Shadow Fall was announced at the PlayStation Meeting in February 2013, and will be a Playstation 4 launch title.5
