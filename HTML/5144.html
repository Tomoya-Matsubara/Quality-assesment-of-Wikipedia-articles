Qarah Guyoz (, also Romanized as Qarah G<U+016B>yoz and Qareh G<U+016B>yoz)1 is a village in Torjan Rural District, in the Central District of Saqqez County, Kurdistan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 582, in 105 families.2
