The Fallowfield Loop railway line was a local railway route in Greater Manchester, England. Trains on the Manchester, Sheffield and Lincolnshire Railway line (later, the Great Central Railway line) used the 'Loop' to connect to Manchester Central railway station (now the Manchester Central conference centre).
It was built in 1891 and closed in 1988.
