Raven (born David Petruschin,1 April 8, 1979) is an American drag queen and reality television personality from Riverside, California.2 She is best known as a contestant on Season 2 of RuPaul's Drag Race.3. She was first runner up of season 2,4 and is slated to appear on RuPaul's Drag U in the summer of 20105 along with fellow season 2 queens Morgan McMichaels, Pandora Boxx, and Jujubee.6

RuPaul's Drag Race

Raven performed on Drag Race when she was 30 years old. In a Match Game-like episode of the show, she humorously impersonated billionaire heiress Paris Hilton. Raven won her first mini-challenge in the first episode "Gone with the Window" by winning the photo shoot challenge. Her next win came in the "Here Comes the Bride" episode in which the queens had to decorate a box with something old, something new, and something borrowed, showing their personal style. Raven borrowed Jujubee's raven hat, securing a win.7 Raven also won the main challenges in the seventh8 and eighth9 episodes "Once Upon a Queen" and "Golden Gals" respectively. In the seventh episode, the queens had to create and promote their autobiographies. Raven's book, Young, Broke, and Fabulous was the winner.10 In episode 8, the contestants were tasked with making over older gentlemen as their "drag mothers." Raven and her drag mother, Golda LaMay,11 won the challenge.12 Although he ended up as the runner-up of the second season's competition, Petruschin has criticized the way he was portrayed on Drag Race, and felt the show's editors purposely edited the show's segments in order to portray him as a villain.

In addition to his participation in RuPaul's Drag Race was featured on an episode of America's Next Top Model, Cycle 8, in which he participated in a photo shoot with each of the show's contestants in male drag and paired with a drag queen.

Personal life

Petruschin currently resides in Riverside, California with his dog Blu.

He previously worked as a cosmetics salesperson and as a freelance makeup artist before pursuing drag career on a full-time bases. Currently he hosts several drag-related events at Oasis nightclub in Upland, California.
