The Mississippi Beach Kings were an indoor soccer team based in Biloxi, Mississippi. They played their games in the Mississippi Coast Coliseum. They were members of the Eastern Indoor Soccer League and played only during the 1998 season. During the 1997 season, the team played in Columbus, Georgia as the Columbus Comets.1 During their existence, the Beach Kings/Comets played a total of 52 games, winning 23, two via shootout, and losing 35, four via shootout. They scored a total of 595 goals and allowed a total of 719 goals and notched 65 total points in standings out of a possible 156 points.2

History

1997 season

The Columbus Comets finished 7th overall in the 7 team league. They finished with a record of 3 wins, 21 losses and 3 shootout losses for 12 points.3

1998 season

The Mississippi Beach Kings finished 2nd overall in the 7 team league. They finished the season with a record of 18 wins, 10 losses, 2 shootout wins and 1 shootout loss for 53 points. The Beach Kings made the playoffs, defeating the Huntsville Fire 2 games to 1 in the semi-final round. In the championship game, the Beach Kings lost to the Lafayette SwampCats 10-9.4
