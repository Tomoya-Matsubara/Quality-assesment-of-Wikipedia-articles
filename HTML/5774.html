Chubar (, also Romanized as Ch<U+016B>bar, Choobor, Chowbar, and Chuber; also known as Chobar)1 is a village in Chubar Rural District, Ahmadsargurab District, Shaft County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,248, in 335 families.2
