Mar<U+00ED>a Cristina Arango Vega is the widow of the 31st President of Colombia, Misael Pastrana Borrero, and served as First Lady of Colombia from 1970 to 1974. She is the mother of the 38th President Andr<U+00E9>s Pastrana Arango.

Personal life

Mar<U+00ED>a Cristina was born in Bogot<U+00E1> to Carlos Arango V<U+00E9>lez, a Liberal party politician, and his wife Mar<U+00ED>a Vega Jaramillo.1

In 1950, while her father was serving as Ambassador of Colombia to the Holy See, she met Misael Pastrana Borrero, who was working as a diplomat attached to her father's Embassy.2 They got engaged that year and married on 24 Febraury at the Saint Bartholomew School's Madonna Della Strada Chapel in Bogot<U+00E1>.3 Together they had four children, Juan Carlos, Andr<U+00E9>s, Jaime, and Mar<U+00ED>a Cristina.
