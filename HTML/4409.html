Sefkerin () is a village in Serbia. It is situated in the Opovo municipality, in the South Banat District, Vojvodina province. The village has a Serb ethnic majority(86.75%) and its population numbering 2,627 people (2002 census).

Historical population

  • 1961: 3,028
  • 1971: 2,837
  • 1981: 2,836
  • 1991: 2,717
